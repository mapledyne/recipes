Evolution Cucumber Salad

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
2 cucumber, english
sea salt
pepper , black
6 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
2 tablespoons lemon
a few sprigs mint, fresh, roughly chopped
large tablespoon yogurt, plain, full fat organic preferred

Instructions:
Peel 2 cucumbers, chop off the ends and halve them lengthwise. Gently scoop out the seeds and discard. Chop the cucumber into irregular bite-sized chunks and place in bowl.
Season with a good pinch of sea salt and freshly ground pepper. Add 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil and 2 tablespoons lemon juice. Mix well.
Pick some leaves off a few sprigs of fresh mint and roughly chop them. Add to salad and toss together.
Add large tablespoon natural yogurt to the salad. Toss until well coated and serve drizzled with a little extra virgin olive oil.
