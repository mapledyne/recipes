Carrot Coins, Cumin Ginger

Category : Kid Friendly, Side Dish, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 lb carrots, organic - wide diameter, peeled and sliced into coins about 1/8-inch thick
2 tablespoons butter, unsalted
1/2 teaspoon cumin, ground
1/2 teaspoon ginger, ground
salt
pepper
1/4 cup water

Instructions:
Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high until bubbling. Add the carrots and cook, stirring occasionally, until the pieces are beginning to brown.
Add the cumin, ginger and salt to taste, and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add the water, reduce the heat to low and cover. Cook 5 to 10 minutes, or until the carrots are as tender as you would like. Uncover, add pepper and more salt if needed, and serve.
