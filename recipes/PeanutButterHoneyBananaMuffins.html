Peanut Butter Honey Banana Muffins

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 20

Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, salted, softened
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup butter, creamy peanut, no-stir natural
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cup bananas, mashed
3/4 cup milk, whole
1 1/4 cups flour, whole wheat
1 cup flour, white whole wheat or all-purpose
2 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
salt
1/3 cup peanut butter or chocolate chips, optional

Instructions:
1.Preheat oven to 375. Line muffin tin with cupcake liners or spray tin with non-stick cooking spray.
2.In the bowl of a stand mixer or with a hand mixer, beat together butter and honey until creamy. Add in peanut butter, eggs, vanilla, bananas, and milk.
3.In a separate bowl, whisk together flours, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until combined. Stir in peanut butter chips, if desired.
4.Pour batter into muffin tin, filling each cup about Â¾ full. Bake for 8-11 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
5.Remove to a wire rack to cool. Store leftovers in an airtight container.
