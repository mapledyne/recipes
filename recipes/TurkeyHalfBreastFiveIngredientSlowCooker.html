Turkey Half Breast, Five-Ingredient Slow Cooker

Category : Meat, Slow or Pressure Cooker

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
4 lb bone-in turkey breast
1/2 Tablespoon olive oil, extra virgin
salt
lemon pepper
3 shallots, halved
4 garlic cloves, peeled and smashed
1/2 cup vegetable broth

Instructions:
1.Rinse turkey and pat dry with paper towels.
2.Rub olive oil over turkey. Sprinkle with salt and lemon pepper.
3.In a skillet on medium-high heat, brown the turkey breast, meaty side down.
4.Place, meaty side up, in slow cooker.
5.Place shallots and garlic around side of cooker.
6.Cover and Cook on low 8 hours,
7.Remove from slow cooker and let stand 10 minutes before slicing
