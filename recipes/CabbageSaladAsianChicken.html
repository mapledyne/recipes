Cabbage Salad, Asian Chicken

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 green cabbage (or one bag coleslaw mix)
3 - 4 chicken breasts or thighs, poached or grilled diced (about two-three cups)
1 peppers, bell red, diced
1/2 cup almonds (optional), slivered toasted
1/4 cup sesame seeds (black or white), toasted
2 bags ramen snack
1 bunch green onions, sliced
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon sesame oil
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ginger, fresh, minced
Â¼ cup cilantro, basil and/or mint (get one big bunch or two small), chopped

Instructions:
Mix together all salad ingredients in bowl. Mix dressing and pour over ingredients. Serve immediately.
If you want to prep the salad ahead of time, leave out the ramen snack, go ahead and dress the salad, refrigerate, then add the ramen at the last minute.
