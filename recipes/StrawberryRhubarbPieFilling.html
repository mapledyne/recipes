Strawberry Rhubarb Pie Filling

Category : Dessert, Fruit, Pie etc.

Description:
From my pie ninja class

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 50 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
3 cups rhubarb, fresh
3 cups strawberries, fresh, wild if available
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 vanilla bean
1/4 cup cornstarch
1 tablespoon Grand Marnier
pinch of salt, kosher

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees and bake the bottom pie crust.
Split and scrape the seeds from the vanilla bean pod. Rub the vanilla bean into the sugar until evenly dispersed.
Mix everything together in a medium bowl and pour into a pre-cooked pie shell.
Top with pie crust or crumb topping and bake 40 - 60 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
