Croutons, Cute Buttered

Category : Salad, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
bread, white close crumbed (like brioche, challh, pain de mie or plain stale sliced), 1/2 inch cubed
butter, melted
salt

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Cut bread into cubes. Toss with melted butter and a little salt.
Spread on a baking sheet and toast for 7 minutes in the oven. Stir them around with a spatula and bake for a couple minutes more as necessary. When they are crisp through and nicely tanned they are done.
Sprinkle on vegetable soups, scrambled eggs, boiled vegetables or saut√©ed greens.
