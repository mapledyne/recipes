Squash, Roasted Acorn with Maple and Red Pepper Flakes

Category : Veggie Side

Description:
This is a song about a fall-y goard-y side dish. The name says it all. It's easy, and it's sweet, and it's spicy, and you want it. Fa la la la la. End of song.

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 50 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
1 squash, medium acorn, cored and cut into wedges
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup (scant) maple syrup
pinch of salt
pinch of red pepper flakes

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400Â° F.
2. Toss the squash wedges in a large bowl with olive oil and maple syrup.
3. Spread into one layer on a baking sheet, and then sprinkle with salt and red pepper flakes. Roast for 40 to 50 minutes or until squash is cooked through.
