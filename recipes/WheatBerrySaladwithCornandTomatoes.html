Wheat Berry Salad with Corn and Tomatoes

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups wheat berries [8560], cooked
1 small onion, sweet, chopped fine
2 garlic cloves, minced and mashed to a paste with 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup vinegar, balsamic
1/3 cup olive oil, extra virgin
6 scallions (green onions), chopped fine
1 1/2 cups (or about 2 ears) corn
1 pint tomatoes, cherry or grape
1/2 cup chives, fresh, chopped
1/2 cup parsley, fresh, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
While wheat berries are cooking, in a large bowl stir together onion, garlic paste, vinegar and oil. Drain grains well and add to onion mixture. Toss mixture well and cool.
Add scallions, corn, tomatoes, chives and parsley. Toss well and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Salad may be made 1 day ahead and shilled, covered. Bring salad to room temperature before serving.
