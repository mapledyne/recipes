Cheese and Spinach Muffins [Baby-Led Weaning, page 167]

Category : Snacks, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 10

Ingredients:
butter, unsalted
1/2 onion, red , small
1/2 cup plus 3 tablespoons flour, all purpose
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 eggs, large
1/2 cup milk, whole, organic preferred
2/3 cup cheese, grated
2 cups spinach, baby, organic preferred, in bag
1/2 cup flour, whole wheat

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Grease the muffin pan.
Heat the oil or butter in a frying pan, add the onion, and fry until soft, then lift out using a slotted spoon (to drain off the oil) and set aside.
Sift the flours, baking powder and cayenne pepper into a bowl and make a well in the center. Beat the egg in a liquid measuring cup and add the milk, whisking them together, then pour the mixture into the flour and fold together.
Add the cheese, fried onion, spinach, and fold gently until evenly mixed, then spoon the mixture into the muffin tray.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes (depending on size), until golden brown and springy, then remove from the oven and allow to cool in the muffin tin for a few minutes before turning out.
