Snow, Sensory

Category : Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups baking soda
1/4 cup shampoo, preferrably white

Instructions:
Use your hands to mix these two ingredients (6:1 ratio)! The mixture will soon take on a crumbly, and less sticky, texture, which means your snow is ready for play!
For extended fun, add food coloring to create vibrantly colored snow, find small toys to create an arctic habitat in the snow, or build mini snowmen with tiny button accessories!
