Barley with Mushrooms and Parmesan, Savory

Category : Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 5

Ingredients:
2 cups (475 ml) vegetable broth
2 cups (475 ml) water
1 cup barley, pearled
2 cups (120 g) crimini mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons thyme, fresh
1 teaspoon salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
3/4 teaspoon pepper, cracked black
1/2 cup cheese, Parmesan
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
salt, flaky
optional red pepper flakes
1 - 5 eggs, large (optional), to poach and serve on top

Instructions:
In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, bring the broth and water to a low boil over medium heat. Stir in the barley, mushroom, thyme, kosher salt, and black pepper and return to a low boil. Decrease the heat to low and cover.
Let the barley cook until most of the liquid is absorbed yet it's still creamy, 60 to 65 minutes. The barley will continue soaking up liquid once it's removed from the heat, so it's okay if it's a little loose when you pull it off the burner.
Let it sit, covered, off the heat for 10 minutes (poach the eggs during this time if using), then stir in the Parmesan. Taste and season with additional salt and black pepper as desired. Serve hot, drizzled with the olive oil, topped with the optional poached egg, and sprinkled with a bit of flaky salt and red pepper flakes.
