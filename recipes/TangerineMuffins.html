Tangerine Muffins

Category : Breakfast, Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 24

Ingredients:
4 cups flour, all-purpose
1 cup sugar
4 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups yogurt, vanilla
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup butter
2 cups oranges, tangerine, diced
2 tablespoons tangerine peel, (optional) grated

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Grease or paper line 2 muffin pans.
3. In large bowl combine dry ingredients and mix.
4. In small bowl combine the yogurt, egg, butter and milk, stir until smooth.
5. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until completely mixed together, then add tangerine pieces and optional peel.
6. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full and bake for 18 - 20 mins. Test with a toothpick, if clean they are ready.
7. Cool for 5 minute before removing from pans.
