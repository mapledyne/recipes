Greek Fennel Skillet

Category : Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 fennel bulb, julienned
1 onion, large, julienned
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 28 oz can tomatoes, canned, diced
1 1/2 cup cheese, feta, crumbled
1 can olives, black
1 can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
salt
pepper

Instructions:
In a medium frying pan, sauté olive oil and garlic for one minute.
Add fennel bulbs and onion and add to sauté until tender, 5-10 minutes.
Add lemon juice and tomatoes and cook over medium heat until part of liquid evaporates, 10 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in feta cheese, black olives, and chickpeas.
