Bunkhouse Dressing

Category : Salad, Sauce

Description:
Good salad dressing especially if olive oil is of low quality.

Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 garlic clove, pounded
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon or lime juice or a mix of the two
yogurt, plain, whole milk
2 tablespoons oil, canola or extra virgin olive oil
(optional) herbs, fresh such as basil, mint and cilantro

Instructions:
Mix the garlic, salt, lemon juice and yogurt in s small bowl.
Add the oil in slowly, stirring all the while to mix it well and keep the dressing from breaking - if it starts to separate, stir faster and pour slower.
