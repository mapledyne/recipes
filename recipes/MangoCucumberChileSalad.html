Mango Cucumber Chile Salad

Category : Fancy, Fruit, Salad, Veggie Side

Description:
This salad was easy to put together and really pretty. The cucumber and mango were a tasty pair and the herbs, chili, and lime juice added a lot of flavor. To punch it up a little, I added some sliced avocado and a bit more salt. It was a nice partner for some grilled salmon on a hot summer night.

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1 mango, very ripe, sliced
2 cucumbers, English, thinly sliced into rounds
1/4 teaspoon (or to taste) chile powder, cayenne
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons scallions, thinly chopped
2 tablespoons herbs (mint, cilantro, basil, chives, chopped fresh or a mix
1 lime

Instructions:
Arrange the mango and cucumber slices on a plate. Evenly sprinkle chile and salt over. Top with the chopped scallions and herbs, then cut lime in half, and squeeze juice evenly over everything.
