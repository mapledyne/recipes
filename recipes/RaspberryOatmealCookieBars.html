Raspberry Oatmeal Cookie Bars

Category : Dessert

Description:
"One of my favorite cookie bar recipes." - Author

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 40 Min
Servings: 1

Ingredients:
1 cup sugar light brown packed
2 cups flour, all-purpose
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
salt
2 cups oats rolled
1 teaspoon cinnamon, ground
1 egg
3/4 cup butter, salted, softened
1 1/2 cup raspberry jam seedless

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 13 x 9 inch pan, and line with greased foil.
2. Combine brown sugar, flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and rolled oats. Rub in the egg and butter using your hands or a pastry blender to form a crumbly mixture. Press 2 cups of the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Spread the jam to within 1/4 inch of the edge. Sprinkle the remaining crumb mixture over the top, and lightly press it into the jam.
Optional: Sprinkle chocolate or white chocolate chips over top.
3. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in preheated oven, or until lightly browned. Allow to cool before cutting into bars.
