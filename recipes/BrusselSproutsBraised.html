Brussel Sprouts, Braised

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 3 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, extra-virgin
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
2 cups brussel sprouts, frozen, thawed and halved lengthwise
to taste Salt and freshly cracked black pepper
1/2 cup water

Instructions:
In a large skillet, heat the butter and oil over medium-high heat until the butter foams. Add the thawed brussel sprouts and cook until lightly browned, turning once or twice and sprinking with salt and pepper (about 5 mins).
Add the water, cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 10 minutes or until the sprouts are tender but not mushy. Uncover, boil off any remaining water, and serve.
