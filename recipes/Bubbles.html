Bubbles!

Category : Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 tablespoons light karo syrup or glycerin
4 tablespoons dish detergent (Joy or Dawn work the best)

Instructions:
Mix everything together and let sit for at least four hours. Doubling and tripling the recipe works well if you need lots.
