Balsamic Vinaigrette Pearls

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
240 ml (1 cup) olive oil, extra virgin
120 ml (1/2 cup) balsamic vinegar
2g (about 1 scant teaspoon) agar-agar powder
2 tomatoes (optional)
basil (optional)
mozzarella cheese (optional), diced

Instructions:
An hour ahead:
Put the olive oil in a tall drinking glass or beaker and put it in the freezer for 45 min to an hour. It should every cold and just starting to turn opaque and thicken slightly when you begin the next section.
Directions:
In a small sauce pan, combine the balsamic vinegar and agar-agar and bring to a boil.
Remove the olive oil from the freezer.
Slightly tip the saucepan and fill the syringe with the vinegar mixture.
Holding the syringe horizontal, dribble the vinegar mixture a drop at a time into the cold drinking glass.
When you made as much as you'd like, pour the oil and spheres through a sieve set over a bowl or glass, drain well, then rinse gently under cool running water. Alternately, scoop out spheres a few at a time with a slotted or pierced spot and rinse gently in a bowl of cool water, then drain.
Serve or store in a container I the fridge until ready to use.
To Serve:
Slice a tomato, season with salt and pepper, then scatter the pearls and mozzarella over the whole thing.
Alternately, put in the well of an avocado cut in half seasoned with coarse sea salt. Don't rinse the pearls to keep some of the olive oil.
Alternately, use on a salad. No need to rinse since the oil here either.
