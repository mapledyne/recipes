Salad Dressing, Thai

Category : Salad, Sauce

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons fish sauce
3 tablespoons lime juice, fresh
2 teaspoons sugar
3 chilies, thai
2 cloves garlic, minced to a paste

Instructions:
Stir together all ingredients until sugar is dissolved.
Serve over steak, grilled chicken, cucumbers, salmon - seriously this stuff is good on everything.
