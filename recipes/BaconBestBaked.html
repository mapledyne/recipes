Bacon, Best Baked

Category : Breakfast, Kid Friendly, Meat

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
bacon, Nueske's brand. Cherrywood smoked, green package (exact)

Instructions:
Heat your oven to 400° F, put slices of bacon on a baking sheet—as many as you'd like, just make sure they fit in one layer—and slide it into the oven.
The bacon will sizzle in its own rendered fat, cooking evenly. Just 15 minutes or so later, you'll have those perfect little pork ribbons—with minimal cleanup.
