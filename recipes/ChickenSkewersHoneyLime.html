Chicken Skewers, Honey-Lime

Category : Grilled, Kid Friendly, Meat, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 lime lime juice, fresh
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 - 2 teaspoons hot sauce, Sriracha
to taste red pepper flakes
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
1 pound chicken breast, boneless, skinless

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl or bag combine all marinade ingredients. Mix thoroughly.
2. Submerge chicken breast pieces and marinate for at least one hour.
3. Thread onto skewers. Grill on medium heat 6 - 8 minutes per side until juices run clear.
