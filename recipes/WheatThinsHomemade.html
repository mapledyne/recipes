Wheat Thins, Homemade

Category : Appetizer, Kid Friendly, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1.5 cups oatmeal, uncooked
1 cup white flour, unbleached (you may substitute 1/2 cup of whole wheat flour for the white)
1/2 cup wheat germ
1.5 tbsp sugar
(not necessary - omit if you choose) salt
1/3 cup oil
1/2 cup water

Instructions:
Mix all together, and roll out onto cookie sheet. Sprinkle with salt. Lightly roll again to press salt in. Cut into squares or diamonds - a pizza cutter works well. Bake at 350F.
After 15 minutes begin checking the crackers and remove the ones that are golden brown and hard. Continue checking every 3-5 minutes.
Optional: sprinkle with sesame or poppy seeds before baking.
