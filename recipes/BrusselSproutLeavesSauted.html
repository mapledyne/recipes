Brussel Sprout Leaves, Sauted

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 lb brussel sprouts, trimmed
to taste salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
1 tablespoon olive oil, extra-virgin
1 tablespoon butter, unsalted
1 tablespoon vinegar, sherry

Instructions:
Using a paring knife and working with 1 sprout at a time, insert the tip of the knife just outside the stem end of the sprout and twist the sprout around the knife to release the core. Using your fingers, separate individual leaves; discard cores.
Bring a 4 quart saucepan of salted water to a boil. Cook leaves 1 minute and transfer to an ice bath until cold.
Drain and dry leaves completely using paper towels.
Heat oil and butter in a 12" skillet over high heat. Cook leaves, stirring occasionally, until golden and slightly crisp, 6 - 8 minutes. Stir in vinegar and salt
