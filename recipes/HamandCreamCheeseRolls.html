Ham and Cream Cheese Rolls

Category : Appetizer, Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 package ham, cooked, square deli slices (exact)
1/2 package cream cheese

Instructions:
A Ball family favorite.
Dry the ham (spread out on paper towel works well). Spread cream cheese evenly on the ham into each corner.
Roll it up.
Cut 1/2 slices from the roll, secure with a toothpick.
