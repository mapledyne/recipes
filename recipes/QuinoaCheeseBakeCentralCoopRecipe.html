Quinoa Cheese Bake [Central Coop Recipe]

Category : Casserole, Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 25 Min
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa, white, organic preferred
2 tablespoons butter, unsalted
1 1/2 cup milk,
1/4 cup flour, all purpose
1 cup cheese Cheddar (Tillamook, sharp), grated
1/2 cup onion, sweet, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup cauliflower, purple, orange or white, organic preferred, chopped
small package spinach, frozen, organic preferred

Instructions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Cook the pasta according to package directions, drain and set aside.
In a pan, heat butter until melted. Whisk in flour until combined, then whisk in milk. To prevent clumping, continue stirring until the sauce thickens. Add half of the cheese, the onion, garlic, cauliflower, and spinach. Stir to combine. Salt and pepper to taste.
Pour quinoa and mixture into an oven-safe dish and top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes.
