Carrot Salad, Spicy

Category : Salad, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 1 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons sugar, brown
1 tablespoon lemon juice, fresh
2 teaspoons paprika, sweet
1 teaspoon cumin, ground
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon cayenne, (optional)
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup olive oil
1 1/2 lb (about 8 carrots) carrots
2 teaspoons cilantro, chopped for garnish

Instructions:
Whisk together sugar, lemon juice, spices (omit cayenne for a non-spicy version), salt, and oil in a large bowl until sugar is dissolved.
Using a veggie peeler or mandolin, shave carrot slices and blanch in a pot of boiling salted water about 30 seconds, until crisp tender. Drain well. Immediately toss with dressing and chill, covered.
Before serving, sprinkle with chopped fresh cilantro and/or mint.
