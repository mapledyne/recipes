Toasted Ravioli

Category : Appetizer, Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time: 45 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
3/4 to 1 pound ravioli, fresh
3 eggs, large, beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
2 cups breadcrumbs
3 tablespoons parsley, fresh, finely chopped
1 teaspoon rosemary, fresh, finely chopped
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
Vegetable oil, for frying
1/2 cup cheese, parmesan
Marinara sauce, for dipping

Instructions:
1. Whisk the eggs and milk in a shallow dish or pie plate. Mix the breadcrumbs, parsley, rosemary and 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper in another shallow dish.
2. Dip the ravioli in the egg mixture, letting the excess drip off, then coat with the breadcrumb mixture. Return to the baking sheet and freeze until hard, about 15 minutes.
3. Heat about 1 inch vegetable oil in a deep skillet until a deep-fry thermometer registers 350 degrees F. Fry the ravioli in 2 or 3 batches, turning as needed, until golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes per batch. Transfer to a paper towel-lined baking sheet to drain and immediately sprinkle with the parmesan. Serve with the marinara sauce.
