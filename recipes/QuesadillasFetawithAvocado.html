Quesadillas, Feta with Avocado

Category : Kid Friendly, Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
Olive oil or butter
1 package tortillas, flour or corn
Feta cheese
1 avocado
Hot sauce (for Bria), optional
pico de gallo or salsa, fresh

Instructions:
1. Sandwich a generous amount of feta cheese between two corn (or flour) tortillas. In a large saut√© pan, heat a little olive oil or butter over medium-high heat. Add in your quesadillas, then watch them, turning several times until the cheese is melted and a nice browning occurs.
2. Cut them into quarters and serve with the fresh pico de gallo and sliced avocado on top. You could add a dollop of sour cream, serve them on a bed of greens, and/or drizzle them with some hot sauce. Very easy, fresh, and fast.
