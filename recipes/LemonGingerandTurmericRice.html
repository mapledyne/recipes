Lemon, Ginger, and Turmeric Rice

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Side Dish, Slow or Pressure Cooker

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
rice, brown
1 lemon
1 inch piece ginger, fresh
extra-virgin olive oil
1 pat butter
1 heaped tablespoon turmeric
salt, sea
black pepper, freshly ground

Instructions:
Cook your rice.
Zest your lemon, then cut it in half.
Peel and grate that ginger.
Put a large frying pan on a low heat and add a lug of olive oil and the butter.
When it starts to bubble gently, add the lemon zest, ginger, and Tumeric and squeeze over the lemon juice.
Season with salt and pepper.
Stir until you have a golden paste.
Spoon your cooked rice into the frying pan. Stir thoroughly and serve.
