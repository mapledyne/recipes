Cheese and Potato Quiche

Category : Needs Work

Description:


Prep Time: 25 Min
Cook time: 45 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 pie crust unbaked
6 eggs, at room temperature
2 cups cheese, Gruyere, Cantal, or chedder, grated
2 cups half-and-half cream, heated gently until warm
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
2 cups potato
rosemary or dill

Instructions:
Boil potatoes in salted water until you can easily pierce with a fork. Season with rosemary or dill. Allow to cool to room temperature.
Heat the oven to 425°F and set the rack in the middle. Prebake the chilled crust until the crust begins to brown, 10-12 minutes. Remove and let cool on a rack while you prepare the filling. Reduce the oven temperature to 325°F.
Combine the remaining ingredients and beat until well blended. Put the semi-cooked shell on a baking sheet and pout in the egg mixture. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until almost firm (it should still jiggle just a little in the middle) and lightly browned on top; reduce the oven heat if the shell's edges are darkening too quickly. Cool on a rack; serve warm or at room temperature.
Pesto Quiche - Substitute 3/4 cup pesto for 1/2 cup each of the cheese and cream.
