Croutons, Rustic Oily

Category : Salad, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time: 3 Min
Cook time: 7 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
bread, rustic (Bakery Nouveau)
olive oil, extra-virgin
salt

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
Carve the crust off a good loaf of rustic bread with a serrated knife, and tear the loaf into 1/2 inch pieces.
Put them in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt. Toss and repeat until the bread looks coated with oil and tasted good.
Spread on a baking sheet and toast for 7 minutes in the oven. Stir them around with a spatula and bake for a couple minutes more as necessary. When they are brown and tan and still soft inside they are done.
Toss with salad greens, make bread salad, sprinkle over braised meats or use in stuffing.
