Pasta, Baked with Cauliflower

Category : Casserole, Kid Friendly, Pasta, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
2 cups cream, heavy
1/2 cups cheese, pecorino romano or parmesan cheese
1/2 cups cheese, mozzarella
2 tablespoons ricotta
1 1/2 teaspoons salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
12 ounces pasta, penne or farfalle
1 1/2 lbs cauliflower, cut into small florets
4 tablespoons butter, unsalted

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit. Stir together the cream, cheeses, and salt in a large bowl.
2. Boil the pasta and cauliflower together in salted water for four minutes and drain.
3. Toss the drained pasta and cauliflower into the bowl with the cream and cheeses. Divide into four shallow 24-ounce baking dishes (or one larger, 9 x 13 style). Dot the top of each dish with 1 tablespoon of butter, cut into bits. Bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until some of the pasta is well browned and crunchy. Serve immediately.
