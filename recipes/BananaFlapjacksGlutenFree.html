Banana Flapjacks, Gluten-Free

Category : Breakfast, Fruit

Description:
Who doesn't love a stack of pancakes? These healthy, totally gluten-free and vegan ones really hit the spot. Instead of just throwing some sliced bananas on top of these flapjacks, we incorporated banana right into the batter making them both tender and incredibly flavorful.

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
2 banana, peeled and roughly chopped
1 cup milk, almond
1/2 cup flour, gluten-free
1/2 cup flour, almond
1 - 2 eggs
2 teaspoons baking powder
pinches of salt
1 -2 tablespoon oil for frying, (coconut oil is great)
Maple syrup for serving

Instructions:
1. Combine the banana, egg and the almond milk in a blender and puree until smooth. Pour the mixture into a bowl and stir in the flours, baking powder and salt.
2. Heat up the oil in a nonstick skillet set over medium heat. Working in batches if necessary, spoon in the batter (we like small ‘silver dollar pancakes,’ which are each about 2 tablespoons worth of batter). Cook the pancakes until nicely browned, about 2 minutes on each side. Transfer the pancakes to warm plates and serving with plenty of maple syrup.
