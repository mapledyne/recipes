Chicken Cabbage Salad

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
5 tablespoons canola oil
3 tablespoons limes
1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 thai chile, minced
6 cups cabbage, savoy and red, finely shredded
2 carrots, shredded
2 cups chicken, boneless skinless, cooked, shredded
2 oz ramen noodles (optional), crushed
1/4 cup basil, fresh, chopped
1/4 cup cilantro, fresh, chopped

Instructions:
In a large bowl, whisk the canola oil, lime juice, fish sauce and chili. Season with salt and pepper.
Toss in cabbage, carrots, chicken, optional ramen noodles and basil and cilantro.
