Penne with Brussel Sprouts and Bacon

Category : Pasta, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
10 ounces pasta, penne
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra-virgin
4 slices bacon Nueske's brand. Cherrywood smoked, green package (exact)
2 cups (8 ounces) brussel sprouts, frozen, thawed, trimmed and halved
to taste salt and black pepper
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup cheese, Parmagiano Reggiano
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet, cook the bacon in the olive oil until crisp. Remove to a paper-towel lined plate, reserving the fat in the pan.
2. Raise heat to medium-high and add the brussel sprouts. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste and cook until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Add the chicken broth, reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer 10-minutes or until the sprouts are tender, stirring occasionally.
3. Meanwhile, cook the penne. Drain and add to the brussel sprouts along with the bacon, cheese and lemon juice. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste, top with additional cheese and serve.
