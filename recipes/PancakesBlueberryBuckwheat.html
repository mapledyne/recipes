Pancakes, Blueberry Buckwheat

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter, melted and cooled slightly
1/2 cup flour, all-purpose
1/2 cup flour, buckwheat, or substitute kamut flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt, kosher
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup milk
1 egg, large
1/4 cup blueberries, wild

Instructions:
1. Combine the buttermilk and milk in a bowl. Stir the egg into the melted butter, then stir the egg-butter mixture into the buttermilk mixture.
2. Stir together the dry ingredients. Dump the liquid ingredients all at once into the dry ingredients. Whisk until just barely combined.
3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high and grease with butter. Pour as many 4-inch pancakes as will comfortably fit. When they begin to set up, top each pancake with a generous teaspoon of blueberries (it's tempting to use more, but if you do - the center of the pancake won't cook, and raw pancake batter is very gross). Flip and finish cooking.
4. Cook remaining pancakes, greasing pan and reducing heat as necessary. Serve with maple syrup.
