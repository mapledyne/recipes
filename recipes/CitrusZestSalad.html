Citrus Zest Salad

Category : Salad, Sauce

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
1 teaspoon citrus (lemon, orange, grapefruit, lime etc.), zest of
1 tablespoon shallot, small diced
2 teaspoon juice, orange or lemon
1 1/2 teaspoons vinegar, red wine
1/4 teaspoon salt
pepper black, freshly ground
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 avocados, sliced
2 oranges, sliced

Instructions:
1. Create very tiny citrus zest pieces (Use a vegetable peeler to cut strips of skin from the fruit, line up strips and cut them into little tiny sticks and then line up the sticks and cut them into little tiny bits or if you must use a microplane).
2. Mix the shallot, citrus juice, vinegar, salt and pepper with the zest and let sit for 5 minutes to marinate the shallot. Stir in the oil.
3. While the dressing is marinating, slice the avocado and oranges.
This dressing is also great on boiled vegetables or grilled or baked fish fillets.
