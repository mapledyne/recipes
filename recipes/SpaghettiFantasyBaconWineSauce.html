Spaghetti, Fantasy Bacon Wine Sauce

Category : Pasta

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
10 ounces spaghetti, thin dried, or other thin pasta
3 ounces bacon
3 leeks
1/2 onion, yellow
1 handful spinach, baby
2 tablespoons butter
salt and black pepper
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup cream
3 ounces cheese, parmesan, grated

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add your pasta and cook according to the directions on the package. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook your bacon until it begins to brown. Add the chopped leeks, onions, and butter. Season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, for a few minutes, until the vegetables soften.
3. Add the white wine to the pan and cook for a few minutes, until the wine reduces by about half. Add the spinach and stir to wilt.
4. Add the cream and simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes.
5. Toss the pasta with the sauce and serve topped with the grated Parmesan.
