Cheesy Amish Breakfast Casserole

Category : Breakfast, Casserole

Description:
"This hearty casserole has bacon or sausage, eggs, hash browns, and three different cheeses all baked into a comforting breakfast dish, perfect for feeding a crowd."

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 55 Min
Servings: 12

Ingredients:
1 pound bacon or sausage, vegetarian, diced
1 onion, sweet, chopped
4 cups hash brown potatoes, frozen shredded, thawed
9 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups cheese Cheddar (Tillamook, sharp), shredded
1 1/2 cups cottage cheese, small curd, full fat
1 1/4 cups cheese, Swiss, shredded

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir bacon or sausage and onion until meat is evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain. Transfer meat and onion to a large bowl. Stir in potatoes, eggs, Cheddar cheese, cottage cheese, and Swiss cheese. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
3. Bake in preheated oven until eggs are set and cheese is melted, 45 to 50 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
