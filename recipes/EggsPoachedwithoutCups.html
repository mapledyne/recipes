Eggs, Poached without Cups

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time: 1 Min
Cook time: 5 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
eggs
1 teaspoon vinegar, red or white wine
to taste salt, in the water

Instructions:
1. In a skillet, bring 2 inches of water to a boil. Crack the eggs into individual cups or small bowls to make it easier to slip them into the water.
2. Season the water with vinegar and salt. Carefully add the eggs, cover the pan, and turn off the heat. Count 5 minutes and dip them out with a slotted spoon for soft yolks.
