Peach Muffins, Sul Lago

Category : Breads, Breakfast, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 12

Ingredients:
2 cups flour, all-purpose
1 cup flour, whole wheat
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon lemons, zest
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
3/4 cup olive oil, extra-virgin, any kind
1/2 cup applesauce
3 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups sugar
2 cups apples, pitted, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch chunks

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a dozen muffins pans (I used butter, you could probably use oil).
2. In a large bowl mix the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and lemon zest.
3. In a smaller bowl mix the oil, eggs and sugar.
4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and stir until combined. Fold in the peaches.
5. Spoon into muffin tins. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove onto a rack to cool just to the point where you won't burn your mouth eating them, but they're still somewhat warm. Have a completely cool one later in the day to determine which you like better. Then go for a walk or a swim. Or better yet both!
