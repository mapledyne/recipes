Green Beans, Steamed

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 8 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
3 servings green beans
to taste butter
to taste salt

Instructions:
1. Trim green beans.
2. Boil 1/2 inch of water and add green beans to steam, 4 - 8 minutes.
3. Drain, toss with butter and salt and serve.
