Peanut Butter Crispy Brown Rice Bars

Category : Dessert

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 15

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter, unsalted
3/4 cup honey, liquid
3/4 cup brown rice syrup
1 1/2 cups peanut butter, unsweetend, no-stir (Justin's brand preferred)
1/2 cup sesame seeds, toasted
6 cups rice cereal, crispy brown
1 cup peanuts, dry-roasted, chopped
salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)

Instructions:
Butter a 9 x 13-inch baking dish and set aside.
In a saucepan, combine the butter, brown rice syrup, and peanut butter. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until warmed and combined.
In a very large bowl, combine the sesame seeds, cereal, peanuts, and salt. Pour the peanut butter mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until combined. Spoon the mixture into the prepared pan and, using the back of a spatula, press to create a nice, even layer. Refrigerate for at least one hour before cutting into bars in the pan. Cover and store at room temperature for 3 to 4 days.
