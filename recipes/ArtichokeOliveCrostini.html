Artichoke Olive Crostini

Category : Appetizer

Description:
You can make it from things in a well-stocked pantry: a can of artichoke hearts, some capers, a jar or can of good green olives, a clove of garlic and olive oil. It couldn’t be easier and although the hideous shade of green has taunted me in these photos too, it’s so not the point. This stuff is addictive and when you put it out with toasted slices of baguette that have been rubbed with a halved garlic clove, people will ignore almost anything else on the table. You know, until you bust out the gougeres.

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 5 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 garlic clove, peeled and smashed
1 cup olives, large green pitted
1 tablespoon capers, rinsed and drained
1 15-ounce can artichoke hearts, canned, drained
1/4 cup olive oil, extra-virgin
8 large slices bread, crusty

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. In a food processor, process the garlic, olives, capers, artichoke hearts and olive oil to a coarse paste.
2. Toast the bread on the oven rack for 6 minutes, or until crisp and browned. Spread the olive paste thickly over the toasts and serve.
Do ahead: The olive paste can be refrigerated for 2 days. Let it return to room temperature before using.
