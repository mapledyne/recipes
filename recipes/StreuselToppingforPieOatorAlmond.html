Streusel Topping for Pie, Oat or Almond

Category : Dessert, Pie etc.

Description:
From my Pie Ninja class

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 50 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
7 ounces flour, all-purpose
3 ounces sugar, brown
3 ounces sugar
1/4 teaspoon cinammon, ground
1/4 teaspoon salt, kosher
1/2 - 3/4 cups oats, rolled or almonds, chopped
6 ounces butter, unsalted, at room temperature

Instructions:
In a bowl, stir together the flour, brown sugar, sugar, cinnamon, salt and oats or almonds.
Add the butter into the flour mixture and smash together, using your fingers. As the butter warms up, the mixture will begin to come together. Keep smashing the butter in until the mixture has the texture of cookie dough rather than sand.
Store in the fridge or freezer until ready to be baked. When ready to use, break up any large clumps.
