Mini Southwestern Frittatas

Category : Breakfast, Snacks, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
8 eggs
1/4 cup milk
3/4 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoons pepper black, freshly ground
1/8 teaspoon hot sauce, Crystal brand (exact)
1/2 cup roasted chopped green chiles
2 tablespoons onion, sauteed chopped
1/2 cup tomato, chopped seeded and drained
1 cup cheddar cheese, sharp, grated
1/2 cup spinach, baby, chopped

Instructions:
With a whisk, beat eggs, cream, salt, pepper and hot sauce until blended thoroughly and fluffy with air.
Add all other ingredients except for 1/2 cup of cheese. Pour evenly into muffin tin cups.
Top each cup with a little of the remaining cheese. Bake for 16-18 minutes at 375F.
Serve with some good crusty whole-grain bread or fresh cantaloupe slices.
