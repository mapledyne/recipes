Zucchini with Basil and Garlic

Category : Veggie Side

Description:
Melissa Clark writes the New York Times column A Good Appetite, and is the author and co-author of a number of books. I've never made anything of hers I didn't like. This recipe can be served warm or cold.

Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds (4 or 5 small) zucchini, washed, ends trimmed and sliced 1/8 inch thick
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra-virgin
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, (more to taste)
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tablespoon basil or mint, fresh, chopped

Instructions:
Preheat the broiler. In a bowl, toss the zucchini with the oil and salt.
Arrange the slices on two baking sheets in an even layer. Broil until the slices begin to brown, about five minutes. Flip the slices and broil for about five minutes more.
Transfer the zucchini to a bowl and toss with the garlic and mint or basil. Drizzle with additional olive oil and salt to taste.
Yield: 4 servings
