Winter Salad with Citrus and Feta

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vinegar, champagne
1 tablespoon orange juice, fresh
1 tablespoon grapefruit juice, fresh
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice, fresh
1/2 clove garlic, pressed or grated on a Microplane
1/8 teaspoon sea salt, fine grain
pinch of pepper black, freshly ground
pinch of lemon zest, grated
pinch of orange zest, grated
1/4 cup olive oil, extra-virgin
8 large handfuls greens, mixture of winter (such as escarole, lacinato kale, endive, or radicchio)
2 grapefruits, cut into segments
generous handful pistachios, unsalted, coarsely chopped
generous topping Feta, green and yellow package, vacuum sealed, preferred, crumbled
2 avocado, sliced, (optional)

Instructions:
Prepare the Dressing:
In a jar or small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, citrus juices, garlic, salt, pepper and zests. Gradually add the olive oil, whisking to emulsify.
Assemble the Salad:
Combine the greens in a wide bowls and add the dressing to taste. Toss gently and top with grapefruit, pistachios and feta. Add avocado slices if using and serve.
