Plums, Hot Buttered

Category : Dessert, Fruit

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
2 slices Bread , white
butter, salted
4 plums, ripe, dark and juicy
1/3 cup sugar

Instructions:
Butter the bread thickly. Remove the crusts. Halve and pit the plums. Lay the bread in a shallow dish, and sprinkle it with most of the sugar.
Place the plums, flat-side up, on the bread and dot over a few bits of butter. Sprinkle over the remaining sugar. Bake in a preheated 400 Â°F oven for 15-20 minutes, until the butter has melted and the juices from the plums are bubbling. Serve hot, with cream.
