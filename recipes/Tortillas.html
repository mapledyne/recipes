Tortillas

Category : Breads

Description:
From the Mole class from the Pantry.

Prep Time:
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
2 cups masa harina
1 teaspoon salt, kosher
1 1/4 cup water, room temperature

Instructions:
In a large bowl, mix the masa marina and the salt until thoroughly combined.
Slowly add 1 cup of room temperature water, mix the ingredients with a fork and continue adding water until the mixture is the texture of play dough.
Using a scoop, make dough balls (about 1") and place them on a sheet pan. Cover them with a damp cloth to prevent drying out.
Heat a dry griddle or skillet. Using a tortilla press, press the dough into a 4-inch rough. Place in the griddle and cook until the tortilla no longer sticks then turn over once.
Place in a tortillera lined with a clean linen napkin.
