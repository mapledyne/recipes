Strawberry Bread

Category : Breads, Breakfast, Snacks

Description:
Delicious and easy. Works with other fruits too.

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 40 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 cup flour, all-purpose
1/2 cup flour, whole wheat
3/4 cup sugar
2/3 cup applesauce
2 eggs, large
2 teaspoons cinnamon, ground
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup chocolate chips, Guittard brand, gold package (exact), (optional)
1 1/2 cups strawberries, organic preferred, mashed

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 35o degrees.
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, stirring until just combined. Pour into a greased 8-inch/1.5 L loaf pan and bake in a preheated about 40 mins. When it is done, a toothpick inserted into the center should come out clean.
