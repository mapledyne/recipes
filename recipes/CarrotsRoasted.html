Carrots, Roasted

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
carrots, peeled and chopped into similarly sized chunks
olive oil, extra-virgin
salt, coarse sea

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
Peel and cut the carrots. Coat liberally in olive oil.
Spread onto a jelly roll pan (lined with parchment paper or the Silpat). Season well with salt.
Roast for 25 - 40 minutes depending on size of carrots and desired softness.
Optional flavor additions: Balsamic vinegar or brown sugar added with the oil; or lemon juice, lime juice and/or fresh herbs added after roasting.
