Muffins, Jumbo Strawberry and Rhubarb

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time: 35 Min
Cook time: 50 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
3 cups flour, all-purpose
3/4 cup sugar, turbinado
1 tablespoon baking powder
salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons butter, unsalted, melted and cooled slightly
2 eggs, large
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1 3/4 cups strawberries, organic preferred, diced
1 3/4 cups rhubarb, fresh, diced
1 teaspoon lemon zest, finely grated

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees and position the rack in the lower third of the oven. Coat 6 jumbo muffin cups with non-stick cooking spray.
2. In a large bowl, mix the flour with the 3/4 cup of sugar, the baking powder, baking soda and salt.
3. In another large bowl, beat the butter with the eggs until well combined. Fold in the dry ingredients and buttermilk in 2 alternating additions, then fold in the diced strawberries and rhubarb and the grated lemon zest.
4. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin cups and sprinkle the tops generously with sugar. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, until golden on top and a toothpick inserted into the center of the muffin comes out clean. Transfer the muffins to a rack to cool before serving.
Make ahead: the muffins can be stored overnight in an airtight container. Reheat for 5 minutes in a 375 degree oven.
