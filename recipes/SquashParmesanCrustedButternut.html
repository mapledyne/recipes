Squash, Parmesan-Crusted Butternut

Category : Appetizer, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 40 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 squash, butternut, cut in 1/4 inch-thick slices
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 cup bread crumbs, panko
1 cup cheese, grated Parmesan
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg, freshly ground
1 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt, kosher
2 garlic cloves, finely minced
2 tablespoons thyme, fresh, finely chopped
2 tablespoons parsley, Italian flat-leaf, roughly chopped

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400Â° F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Toss the squash with olive oil and kosher salt. Place the squash in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
3. Combine the bread crumbs, Parmesan, nutmeg, pepper, salt, garlic, and thyme in a bowl. Stir to mix well. Sprinkle this all over the squash and roast until tender, about 40 minutes. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm or at room temperature.
