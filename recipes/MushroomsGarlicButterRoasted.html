Mushrooms, Garlic Butter Roasted

Category : Appetizer, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 pound mushrooms, such as cremini or white, halved lengthwise if large
2 tablespoons capers, rinsed and chopped
3 garlic cloves, large, minced
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tablespoons butter, unsalted, cut into pieces
2 teaspoons lemon juice, fresh
1/4 cup parsley, flat-leaf, chopped

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°F with rack in middle. Toss mushrooms with capers, garlic, oil, 1/8 teaspoon salt and several grinds of pepper in a 1 1/2- to 2-qt shallow baking dish.
Top with butter and roast, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are tender and golden and bubbly garlic sauce forms below, 15 to 20 minutes.
Stir in lemon juice and parsley. Serve immediately, with crusty bread on the side for swiping up the juices.
