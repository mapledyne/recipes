Ham and Potato Soup

Category : Soup

Description:
"This is a delicious recipe for ham and potato soup that a friend gave to me. It is very easy and the great thing about it is that you can add additional ingredients, more ham, potatoes, etc and it still turns out great."" - Author

Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups potatoes, diced, peeled
1/3 cup celery, diced
1/3 cup onion, finely chopped
3/4 cup ham cooked, diced
3 1/4 cups water
2 tablespoons chicken bouillon granules
salt
pepper, ground white or black
5 tablespoons butter, unsalted
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk, whole, organic preferred

Instructions:
1. Put the onion in a stockpot with olive oil and cook over medium heat until softening, add celery and soften. Add the potatoes, ham and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then cook over medium heat until potatoes are tender, about 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in the chicken bouillon, salt and pepper.
2. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour with a fork, and cook, stirring constantly until thick, about 1 minute. Slowly stir in milk as not to allow lumps to form until all of the milk has been added. Continue stirring over medium-low heat until thick, 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Stir the milk mixture into the stockpot, and cook soup until heated through. Serve immediately.
