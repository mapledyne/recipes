Yogurt Cups with Roasted Black Cherries and Toasted Millet

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 pound (450 g) cherries, black, fresh or frozen, pitted
1 tablespoon lemon juice, fresh
3 tablespoons honey, liquid
generous pinch of kosher salt
3/4 cups (135 g) millet
3 cups yogurt, plain, full fat organic preferred
2 tablespoons honey, liquid

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
To prep the cherries: In a large bowl, combine the cherries, lemon juice, 3 tablespoons of honey and salt and mix well. Turn out onto a glass pie plate or baking dish so the berries nestle together and cook in their juices.
Bake until the cherries have softened and the juices are beginning to thicken, about 15 minutes. Stire every 5 minutes or so to prevent sticking. Serm warm or at room temperature, or let cool and refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
To toast the millet: Pour the millet into a large, dry skillet over mediuem heat. Toast until fragrant, 4 to 6 minutes, stirring occasionally so that it doesn't burn or stick to the pan. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool.
To assemble the cups: Spoon 1/2 cup of the yogurt into the bottom of each cup. Sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon of the toasted millet and top with a large spoonful of roasted cherries (include a little juice!). Top each with 1/4 cup of the yogurt and another generous spoonful of cherries. Finish each with about 2 tablespoons of the toasted millet and a generous teaspoonful of honey if you'd like. These are best enjoyed shortly after they are made.
If making ahead - wait until serving to combine all of the ingredients.
