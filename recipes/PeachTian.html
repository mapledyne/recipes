Peach Tian

Category : Dessert, Fruit, Side Dish

Description:
Great side dish for duck or lamb or try it with a scoop of ice cream!

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
6 peaches, fresh, preferrably a mixture of yellow and white, finely sliced in half moon shapes
2 - 3 rosemary, fresh sprigs
6 tablespoons honey

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Finely slice the peaches into half moon shapes. Align them in an over proof dish.
Place a few sprigs of rosemary on top, and drizzle them with honey.
Cook for 20 - 25 minutes and serve warm.
