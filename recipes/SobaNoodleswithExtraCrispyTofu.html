Soba Noodles with Extra Crispy Tofu

Category : Salad, Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
15 ounces extra firm tofu
4 ounces soba noodles
2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
splash of brown rice vinegar
15 ounces tofu, extra firm
zucchini, sliced thin or julienned
carrots, sliced thin or julienned
green onions or scallions, diced
shredded gim or dried seaweed (optional)
sesame seeds, sprinkle on top
11/2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce or 1-2 teaspoons sriracha sauce (optional)

Instructions:
1. Prepare the crispy tofu per the recipe.
2. Cook soba noodles according to package directions. Rinse with warm water; drain well and set aside.
3. Combine the soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar and vinegar in a small bowl. Whisk well. Taste and adjust any ingredients if needed. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the soba noodles and mix well. Serve, top with the cut vegetables and crispy tofu arranged around the bowl and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Mix and enjoy!
