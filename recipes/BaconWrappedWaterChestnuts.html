Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts

Category : Appetizer

Description:
"Delicious bacon wrapped water chestnuts." - Author

Prep Time: 40 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 cup sugar, brown, packed
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 cups ketchup
1 pound bacon
2 (8 ounce) cans water chestnuts

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and ketchup.
3. Cut bacon in half. Wrap one slice of bacon around each chestnut. Secure the bacon with a toothpick. Put the bacon wrapped waterchestnuts on a wire rack over a 9X 13 baking pan, and baked them until they were crispy ~ 15 to 20 minutes.
5. Remove from water chestnut wraps from the oven and drain the grease out of the pan. Put the wraps directly in the pan and pour the sauce over the wraps.
6. Bake for 25 to 30 more minutes.
