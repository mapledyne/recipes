Sage and Butter Pasta

Category : Pasta, Sauce

Description:
A family favorite

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 pound fettuccine or spaghetti, fresh
4 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup sage leaves, roughly chopped
cheese, Parmesan
salt
pepper

Instructions:
Put a big pot of cold water on to boil. Add salt. Boil the pasta, stirring frequently.
Meanwhile, heat a skillet to medium. Add 3 tablespoons of butter and when it stops foaming, add the sage leaves and a sprinkle of salt. Stir and let the sage leaves sizzle until they turn a darker green, about 1 minute. Turn the heat to low and add a ladle of pasta water to stop them from getting too dark.
Taste the pasta and when it is done, drain it and add it to the pan with the remaining tablespoon of butter, stirring well and splashing in pasta water to give it some flow. Taste, tweak, and serve with cheese to grate and pepper to grind.
Optional Add-ins:
- Peas added to the pan with or without a little cream added into the sage and butter.
- Roasted butternut squash
- Cauliflower florets lazed in the butter a bit before adding pasta
- Boiled and chopped kale
- Chopped and toasted hazelnuts or almonds
