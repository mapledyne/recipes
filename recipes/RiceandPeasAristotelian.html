Rice and Peas, Aristotelian

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Kid Friendly, Side Dish, Veggie Side, Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:
The Whole is Greater than the Sum of its Parts

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
14-ounce bag peas, frozen
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons onion, chopped
4 cups chicken stock
1 cup rice, arborio
1/2 cup cheese, parmesan, freshly grated

Instructions:
1. In a soup pot or dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and saut√© until it becomes lightly golden. In a separate pot, heat the stock until it reaches a simmer.
2. Once the onion is ready, add 3 cups of the simmering stock, the rice, the bag of frozen peas, and a good pinch of salt. Cover the pot and and cook at a low boil for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is just tender. If the soup is too thick, thin it slightly with the remaining stock; it should be thick but not porridge.
3. When the rice is ready, add the grated Parmesan and taste for salt. Serve.
