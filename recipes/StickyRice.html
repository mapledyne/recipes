Sticky Rice

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Kid Friendly, Side Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 4 H
Cook time: 45 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
2 cups rice, thai white sticky, soaked in ample water for at least 4 hours

Instructions:
1. Soak rice in ample water for at least 4 hours.
2. Drain the sticky rice and wrap it in moist cheesecloth. Place in steamer insert.
3. Bring a couple inches of water to boil in the pot and place the steamer insert in the pot. Cover, reduce heat to medium, and steam 45 minutes, flipping the rice packet over once, halfway through cooking.
4. Unwrap and serve immediately. Sticky rice dries out easily, so keep the serving bowl covered with some damp cheesecloth during dinner.
