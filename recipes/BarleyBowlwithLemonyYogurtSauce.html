Barley Bowl with Lemony Yogurt Sauce

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 45 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups (255 g) barley, cooked, still warm
1 cup (55 g) bean sprouts, any variety (mung bean sprouts are awesome)
1/3 cup (60 g) cheese, cotija or queso fresco
almonds, sliced, sliced, toasted
1/4 teaspoon salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
1 avocado, quite ripe, peeled, pitted and diced
salt
1/2 cup yogurt, plain, full fat organic preferred
1 teaspoon lemon zest, grated
1 teaspoon lemon juice, fresh
1 tablespoon chives, fresh, chopped
pinch of salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Whisk the yogurt, lemon zest and juice, chives and salt together in a small bowl. Set aside.
In a small bowl, stir the barley, sprouts, cheese, almonds and kosher salt together. Scoop into two individual bowls and top with avocado and a few generous spoonfuls of the yogurt sauce. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve.
