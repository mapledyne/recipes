Cuban Style Pork Chops

Category : Meat

Description:


Prep Time: 30 Min
Cook time: 7 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
3/4 cup orange juice, fresh
1/2 cup lime juice, fresh
1/3 cup oregano leaves, coarsely chopped fresh
6 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 cup canola oil
4 (8-ounce) pork chops, butterflied and thinly pounded
Salt
8 (1/4-inch) thick slices Swiss cheese
8 (1/4-inch) thick slices boiled ham
2 dill pickles, whole, Bubbies brand (exact), thinly sliced (need about 16 slices)
2 tablespoons cilantro leaves, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil, extra virgin
black pepper,freshly ground

Instructions:
1. Heat the grill to high.
2. Whisk together 1/2 cup orange juice , 1/4 cup lime juice, 3 tablespoons oregano , the garlic, cumin, and canola oil in a large baking dish . Add the pork and turn to coat. Cover and let marinate for at least 15 minutes and up to 2 hours in the refrigerator.
3. Remove the pork from the marinade and pat dry. Place the chops on a flat surface, cut-side up and season with salt and pepper. Place 1 slice of cheese, 2 slices of ham, a few slices of pickle and another slice of cheese on 1 half of the chop. Fold over brush the top with oil and season with salt and pepper. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
4. Place the chops on the grill , oil side down and grill until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip the chops over and continue grilling until the bottom is golden brown and the cheese has melted, 2 to 3 minutes longer.
5. Whisk the remaining orange and lime juices, oregano, and the cilantro with the olive oil and salt and pepper, to taste. Spoon over the chops and serve.
