Thai Catfish Cakes

Category : Kid Friendly, Meat

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 5

Ingredients:
1 pound catfish fillets
1/4 cup shallot, minced
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1 egg, large
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 teaspoon lemongrass, minced
1 teaspoon ginger, fresh, minced
oil, peanut, for frying
rice, cooked, for serving
chile sauce, Thai sweet, for serving
cabbage or lettuce leaves, for serving

Instructions:
1. Cut the catfish into one-inch chunks and place in a food processor. Pulse until the fish is reduced to a coarse paste, about five one-second pulses.
2. Combine the shallot, cilantro, egg, fish sauce, ginger and lemongrass in a bowl, and stir well to mix. Add the fish paste and stir until well combined (it will be a loose mixture).
3. Heat several tablespoons of peanut oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Form the fish mixture into two-inch patties, adding each fish cake to the pan as you form it. Fry the fish cakes until golden brown, about 2 minutes per side, and serve immediately. The don't hold or reheat well.
Spicy variation: Replace the ginger and lemongrass with 1 tablespoon of red curry paste.
