Quinoa Salad with Chicken, Chili, Lime, and Parsley and

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1/2 chicken, rotisserie
1 can beans, white
2 hot chilies, red, fresh, small, very thinly sliced
2/3 cup quinoa
small bunch parsley leaves
2 limes, juiced and zested
2 - 4 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
salt
black pepper, freshly ground

Instructions:
Rinse the quinoa (if rinsing is needed, check the package), then cook it in boiling salted water until it is tender but still has some bite to it. This will probably take about 15 minutes, but start tasting after 12. Drain.
While the quinoa is cooking, chop the chicken and chop the parsley finely. Mix together the lime zest and juice, and 2 tablespoons of oil. Add all of this mix to the cooked quinoa. Stir and add salt and pepper to taste.
