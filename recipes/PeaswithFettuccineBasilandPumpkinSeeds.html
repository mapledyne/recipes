Peas with Fettuccine, Basil, and Pumpkin Seeds

Category : Pasta

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1/3 cups pumpkin seeds
16 oz fettuccine, fresh
1 1/2 cups green peas, fresh or frozen, shelled
4 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
1 clove garlic, minced
8-10 basil leaves, fresh, torn to shreds
salt
pepper, black , freshly ground

Instructions:
Scatter the pumpkin seeds on a baking sheet and cook under a preheated broiler until lightly brown and fragrant. Cook the pasta, uncovered, in boiling salted water till tender.
Put the green peas and oil in a small pan, add the garlic and the basil, season with a little salt and pepper, and cook over a gentle heat for 10 minutes, or 7 if using frozen peas.
Drain the pasta and toss in a large bowl with the hot peas and their olive oil, and toasted pumpkin seeds.
