Grits or Polenta, Oven Method

Category : Kid Friendly, Side Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 1 H
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 cup grits or polenta
1 tablespoon butter
4 cups water
to taste salt
cheese, Parmagiano Reggiano, optional

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a saucepan, whisk together the grits or polenta and 4 cups of water. Throw in 1 tablespoon of butter. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes. Stir and continue baking until it becomes the desired consistency, about 15 more minutes.
Add salt to taste, additional butter and optional parmesan.
