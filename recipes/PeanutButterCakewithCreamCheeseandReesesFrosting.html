Peanut Butter Cake with Cream Cheese and Reeses Frosting

Category : Dessert

Description:
Begin preparing the cake one day ahead.

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 12

Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
salt
10 tablespoons, (1 1/4 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature
1/2 cup peanut butter, old-fashioned (natural) chunky
1 pound sugar, golden brown
4 eggs, large
1 teaspoon vanilla extract [#8144]
1 cup sour cream, full fat
1 1/2 8-ounce packages cream cheese, room temperature
2 cups powdered sugar, divided
6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla extract [#8144]
3/4 cup chilled heavy whipping cream
Reese's peanut butter cups, coarsely chopped
peanuts, glazed

Instructions:
2.
Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Butter three 9-inch-diameter cake pans with 1 1/2-inch-high sides. Line bottoms with parchment paper. Sift first 4 ingredients into medium bowl. Using electric mixer, beat butter and peanut butter in large bowl until blended. Beat in sugar. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, then vanilla. At low speed, beat in flour mixture in 4 additions alternately with buttermilk in 3 additions.
3. Divide batter among pans and spread evenly. Bake cakes until tester inserted into center comes out clean, about 25 minutes. Cool cakes 5 minutes. Turn out onto racks; peel off parchment. Cool cakes completely.
4.
Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese, 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla in large bowl to blend. Whisk whipping cream and 3/4 cup powdered sugar in bowl until mixture holds medium-firm peaks. Fold into cream cheese mixture in 3 additions; chill until firm but spreadable, about 1 hour.
5. Place 1 cake layer, bottom side up, on 9-inch tart pan bottom. Spread with 1/3 of icing. Place another layer, bottom side up, on work surface. Spread with remaining icing; place atop first layer. Top with remaining cake layer, bottom side up.
6. Spread frosting over top and sides of cake. (Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover with cake dome; chill. Let stand at room temperature 2 hours before continuing.) Press candy and peanuts onto top of cake.
