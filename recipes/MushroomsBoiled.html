Mushrooms, Boiled

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
6 mushrooms, large flat
6 tablespoons butter, salted
salt
black pepper, freshly ground
1/2 lemon

Instructions:
Wipe the mushrooms and brush away any soil. Place the mushrooms on a broiler pan. Put large lumps of butter all over the mushrooms and season each one with salt and pepper and a good squeeze of lemon. Cook under a preheated broiler for 10 minutes, spooning the butter and juices over them from time to time.
