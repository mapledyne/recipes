Slime, Homemade

Category : Kid Friendly

Description:
Find the powder in the bulk section of health food stores. It's a type of fiber used in smoothies and energy drinks and is also used in Metamucil.)

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 tbsp psyllium husk powder
Few drops of food coloring

Instructions:
Heat in a saucepan on medium heat, and stir vigorously with a whisk for two or three minutes. It will become an applesauce consistency, and then let it sit on medium heat until you see bubbles (only a minute or two longer). Stir again, and let it sit once more until you see bubbles. Take off heat and let cool. It will store for a few days if kept in a sealed container.
(Place on a cooking tray for play. Provide plastic animals, recycled materials, or things found in nature, etc to explore with in the slime.)
