Lemon Basil Carbonara

Category : Pasta

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
8 ounces angel hair pasta, or any other long pasta
5 eggs, large, lightly beaten
3/4 cup cheese, Parmesan
1 teaspoon lemon zest, grated
1/8 cup basil, thinly sliced

Instructions:
1.Cook pasta according to package directions omitting salt and fat.
2.Combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl.
3.Toss the pasta with the egg mixture immediately after draining to allow the heat from the pasta to cook the eggs and melt the cheese.
