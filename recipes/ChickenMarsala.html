Chicken Marsala

Category : Meat, Pasta, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
8 ounces mushrooms, fresh button or cremini, stems removed and sliced
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra-virgin, divided
2 chicken breasts, boneless, skinless
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/2 cup Marsala wine, sweet, divided
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup cream, heavy
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons butter, unsalted
1 lb pasta, linguine, wholewheat, wholewheat

Instructions:
1. Boil water for the pasta. Cook in parallel to this recipe prep.
2. In a small pan, saut√© mushrooms in 1 tablespoon of the olive oil until lightly caramelized. Remove mushrooms from heat and set aside in a small bowl.
3. Carefully slice each chicken breast lengthwise, making four thinner pieces of chicken total. Lightly salt and pepper the chicken breasts.
4. Heat remaining olive oil in a 10- to 12-inch skillet and brown chicken breasts on each side until almost done (They will continue to cook in the sauce in Step 7.). Remove from skillet and keep warm.
5. Deglaze the pan with the 1/2 cup Marsala wine. Add the garlic. Continue to cook until wine is reduced to be thick and syrupy (au sec).
6. Add cream and reduce slightly.
7. Nestle chicken breasts into the skillet, add the mushrooms and broth and spoon sauce over top. Simmer, uncovered, until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
8. Transfer the chicken to a serving platter and keep warm.
9. Swirl butter into the sauce remaining in the skillet, season to taste with salt and pepper, and allow to reduce slightly. Add a splash of Marsala wine to the sauce and pour over the chicken. Serve immediately.
