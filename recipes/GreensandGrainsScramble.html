Greens and Grains Scramble

Category : Breakfast, Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
6 eggs, large, beaten
1 tablespoon milk
salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
1 (about 1 tablespoon) onions, green, white and light green parts, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic cloves, minced
1 heaping well-packed cup chard, swiss, chopped
1/2 cup wheat berries, cooked
1 tablespoon chives, fresh, chopped
pepper, ground black
salt, flaky
bread for serving

Instructions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and kosher salt; set aside.
Heat 1 tablespoon of the olive oil in a saute pan over medium heat. Add the green onion and garlic and saute until soft, 1 to 2 minutes. Add the greens, grains, and remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil and saute until the greens are wilted and the grains are warmed through, 3 to 5 minutes.
Decrease the heat to low and pour in the egg mixture, gently stirring to comingle them with the greens and grains. Continue stirring until they're softly scrambled, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from the heat, stir in the chives, and season with salt and pepper.
Serve hot with a sprinkling of flaky salt on top, and delicious bread, english muffins or tortillas alongside.
Optional additions: Grated parmesan cheese or a creamy chevre. Chanterelles sauted in a bit of butter and folded in.
