Baked Rhubard Compote

Category : Christine Friendly, Dessert, Fruit, Sauce

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 lb rhubarb stalks, cut into 2" x 1/2" pieces
1/2 cup (100 grams) sugar, granulated
1/2 zest of orange, cut into long strips with a vegetable peeler
1/2 cup orange juice, fresh
1 vanilla bean, seeds scraped and pod included
2 pieces star anise
pinch cinnamon, ground

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
In a small baking dish, combine all of the ingredients and mix gently to distribute the vanilla seeds.
Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes, or until rhubarb is cooked through and soft.
Remove the foil and cook for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the syrup is reduced to your liking.
