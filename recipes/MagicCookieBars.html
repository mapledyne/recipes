Magic Cookie Bars

Category : Dessert

Description:
"This bar cookie is an old fashioned favorite. Chocolate chips, nuts and coconut are set in a caramelized layer on top of a graham cracker crust." - Author

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 36

Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, salted, melted
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 (14 ounce) can condensed milk, sweetened
2 cups M&Ms, dark chocolate, holiday mix (green and red)
1 1/3 cups flaked coconut
1 cup pecans, in pieces, unsalted, chopped

Instructions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F (325 degrees for glass dish). Coat 13x9-inch baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.
2. Combine graham cracker crumbs and butter. Press into bottom of prepared pan. Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over crumb mixture. Layer evenly with chocolate chips, coconut and nuts. Press down firmly with a fork.
3. Bake 25 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool. Cut into bars or diamonds. Store covered at room temperature.
