Mushroom-Lentil Tacos with Tahini Yogurt Sauce

Category : Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 cup lentils, cooked
2 1/2 cups mushrooms, chopped
2 medium cloves garlic, minced
olive oil, extra-virgin
1 1/2 teaspoons cumin
Salt and pepper
pinch of paprika
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup tahini
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 individual container yogurt, Greek-style, plain
Salt and pepper
2 big handfuls of arugula
6 tortillas or pitas

Instructions:
1. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add mushrooms, season with salt and pepper, and cook down until brown and fragrant, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes.
2. While the mushrooms are cooking, mix the yogurt sauce. Add the lemon juice, tahini, yogurt, and olive oil to a bowl and stir to combine. For a thinner sauce, add more olive oil or water or a mixture of the two until you have reached your desired consistency. I liked it a little thicker.
