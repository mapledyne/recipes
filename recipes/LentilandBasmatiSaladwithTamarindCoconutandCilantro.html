Lentil and Basmati Salad with Tamarind, Coconut, and Cilantro

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Salad, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:
About a month ago, I took a wonderful vegetarian Indian cooking class. Everything we made (lentil sambar, vegetable biryani, potato curry, tomato chutney) was delicious, but the dish that knocked my socks off in terms of subtle, yet amazing flavor was a simple mung bean salad with lemon, coconut, cilantro, black mustard, and curry leaf. Here is my version, using du Puy lentils (the mung beans still had a bite to them so I wanted to use a lentil that held its shape) and basmati rice for a complete protein. I have kept the coconut and cilantro but changed up the tempered dressing using tamarind paste and black caraway seeds for sour and nutty notes. The end result is a refreshing salad that is easy to make, flavorful, and equally delicious the next day. Note: If refrigerating overnight, be sure to allow dish to come to room temperature before serving.

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 5

Ingredients:
For the lentil-rice salad:
1 cup rice, basmati, white, rinsed
1/2 cup lentils, du Puy (also known as French lentils), green, rinsed and picked through
2 1/4 cups water
1/3 cup plus 1 tablespoon cilantro, fresh, chopped including stems
1/4 cup coconut flakes, unsweetened, shredded
1/2 teaspoon salt, to taste
1 1/2 teaspoons lime juice
For the dressing:
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon caraway seeds, black (also known as nigella seeds)
1 1/2 teaspoons tamarind paste (I used Neeraâ€™s brand)
3 teaspoons water
Salt, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a small saucepan, combine rice, lentils and water. Cover and bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until tender and water has evaporated, about 25 minutes. Fluff and transfer to a large bowl to cool.
2. In a skillet, temper dressing by heating vegetable oil over medium heat. Add black caraway seeds and cook for about 1 minute. Seeds should sputter a bit. Remove from heat. Add tamarind paste and water, stirring to combine.
3. Pour dressing over rice and fold to mix thoroughly. Fold in coconut and cilantro. Add lime juice and salt to taste. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve warm or at room temperature. Enjoy!
