Asparagus Miso Soup

Category : Soup

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
3 cups stock, vegetable
3 cups water
1/4 cup miso, white
2 carrots, large, finely diced
8 oz asparagus, cut into 1 inch lengths
1 onion, green

Instructions:
In a large saucepan, bring stock and water to a boil. In a small bowl, whisk miso with 2 tablespoons water until smooth; whisk into pan. Reduce heat to medium low, simmer for 30 minutes.
Add carrots and asparagus; cook for about 5 minutes or until asparagus is tender crisp. Serve sprinkled with green onion.
