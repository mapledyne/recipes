Pea Shoot and Baby Arugula Salad with Meyer Lemon Vinaigrette

Category : Fancy, Salad

Description:
This is a bright greenmarket salad that celebrates the grassy, fresh flavors of spring. Whenever I see pea shoots at the market, I can't resist buying some. Peppery arugula is a nice complement to the pea shoots, and a touch of honey in the snappy lemon vinaigrette echoes their sweetness. If you don't have Meyer lemons, regular lemon juice and zest are just fine.

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 lemon, meyer, Juice and zest of
2 tablespoons vinegar, rice
1 teaspoon honey
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
6 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 cups pea shoots, fresh large, densely packed
4 cups arugula, baby, densely packed

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice and zest, vinegar, honey and salt and pepper. Slowly drizzle in the oil, whisking constantly to emulsify. Set aside.
2. Wash and dry the pea shoots and arugula. If you're using mature arugula, roughly chop it into bite-sized pieces. Put all of the greens in a large bowl.
3. Add about half the dressing, toss gently and taste. If necessary, add more dressing, salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
