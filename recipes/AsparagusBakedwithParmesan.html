Asparagus, Baked with Parmesan

Category : Side Dish, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1 pound asparagus
2 Tbsp olive oil, extra-virgin
Salt and black pepper to taste
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated, loosely packed

Instructions:
1 Preheat oven to 400 F. Break or cut off the woody ends of the asparagus spears.
2 Arrange the asparagus spears on a foil-lined baking sheet and coat with the olive oil. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and the parmesan. Bake until the cheese begins to brown, about 8-10 minutes.
