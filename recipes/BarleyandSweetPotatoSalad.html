Barley and Sweet Potato Salad

Category : Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 1 H 15 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 cup barley, uncooked
3 cups water
pinch salt
1 1/2 tablespoon coconut oil
1 potato, sweet, peeled and diced
1/4 cup mint, fresh, chopped
3 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
2 tablespoons vinegar, champagne

Instructions:
Place saucepan over medium-high heat. Add barley and toast until fragrant, 3-5 minutes, stirring often. (Easy to burn!)
Add water and bring the mixture to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 50-60 minutes, until tender but al dente.
Drain any leftover liquid (use colander), cool to room temperature and set aside.
While the barley is cooking, heat the coconut oil in a saut√© pan over medium heat. When the oil is hot and showing ripples, place a single layer of the sweet potato cubes in the pan and sprinkle with salt cook, stirring only occasionally, until all sides are golden brown, about 7-10 minutes. (More like 15-20).
In a serving bowl, gently mix together the sweet potato fries and the barley. Stir in the fresh herbs, olive oil, and champagne vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the olive oil and herbs to your liking.
Serve at room temperature.
