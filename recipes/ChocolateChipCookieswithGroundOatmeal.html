Chocolate Chip Cookies, with Ground Oatmeal

Category : Dessert

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 30

Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups oatmeal
3/4 cups butter, unsalted
1/3 cups sugar, granulated
1/3 cups sugar, brown
1 egg
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 cups flour, all-purpose
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips, Guittard brand, gold package (exact)

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees farenheit.
1. Grind the oatmeal to a flour consistency in a food processor fitted with a blade attachment, set aside.
2. Cream the butter and sugars until fluffy.
3. Add egg and vanilla and mix well, scraping the bowl frequently.
4. Add the baking soda, baking powder, salt, flour, and oatmeal flour and mix until just combined.
5. Mix in the chocolate chips.
6. Drop generous tablespoons of cookie dough onto the cookie sheets, leaving at least 1-inch of space between each. Bake, rotating halfway, for 8 - 10 minutes. The edges should be brown, but the centers soft and light in color.
