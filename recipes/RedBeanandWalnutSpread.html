Red Bean and Walnut Spread

Category : Appetizer, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
3/4 cup walnuts, toasted
1 15 oz can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup scallions (green onions), chopped
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
2 tablespoons vinegar (apple cider or red wine)
1 tablespoon dill, fresh, chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper, ground
optional 1 tablespoon cilantro, fresh, chopped

Instructions:
In a food processor, whirl the toasted walnuts until finely ground. Add the beans, garlic, scallions, olive oil, vinegar, dill, salt, pepper, and cilantro (if using). Pur√©e until very smooth. If it's too thick, add a little water.
