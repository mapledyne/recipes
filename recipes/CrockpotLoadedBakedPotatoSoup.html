Crockpot Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Category : Slow or Pressure Cooker

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
30 oz bag hash browns, frozen
28 oz broth, chicken
10.75oz can soup, cream of chicken
2 tablespoons onion, dried, minced
1 teaspoon black pepper, ground
8oz cream cheese, softened
4-6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 cups cheese, cheddar, shredded

Instructions:
Mix frozen hash browns (no need to thaw), chicken broth, cream of chicken soup, dried minced onion, and pepper in a crockpot and cook on low for 5 hours.
Remove lid and stir in cream cheese. Cook another 30 minutes. Serve with bacon and cheddar on top.
