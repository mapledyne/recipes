Fruit Crisp

Category : Dessert, Pie etc.

Description:
Instead of peaches, you can substitute: apples, peeled, cored, and sliced (increase cinnamon to 1 tsp); plums, pitted and sliced; pears, cored and sliced; or fresh berries.

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
6 cups apples, peeled and sliced crosswise 1/4 inch thick
1 tbsp lemon juice
3/4 cup oats, rolled
3/4 cup sugar, brown, packed
1/2 cup flour, all-purpose
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:
Place peaches in a buttered 8-inch square pan. Sprinkle lemon juice on top.
In small bowl, mix together rolled oats, brown sugar, flour, butter, and cinnamon using pastry blender or fingertips. Sprinkle mixture evenly over peaches and pat down lightly.
Bake in 375 degree oven for 30-40 minutes or until peaches are tender and crumb topping is evenly browned.
