Poached Chicken

Category : Meat

Description:
From the Mole class at the Pantry.

Prep Time:
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
4 chicken, boneless skinless, cooked
1 quart stock, chicken
3/4 cup vinegar, white wine
2 tablespoon salt, kosher
2 cloves, whole
2 sprigs parsley
2 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon peppercorns, black, whole
1 1/2 quart water

Instructions:
In a small saucepan, bring 1 quart of water to a boil.
Preferably (but optional): put bay leaves and peppercorn into a sachet.
In a wide pot, mix the stock with 1/2 quart of water, the vinegar, salt, garlic, herbs, and spices. Bring to a boil, turn the heat to low and add the chicken. It should be covered; if not, add the boiling water until covered.
Simmer the chicken for 15 minutes, cover the pot, turn off the heat and leave the chicken in the liquid for 10 more minutes.
Remove from the liquid and server ore refrigerate for use at another time.
