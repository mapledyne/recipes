Spinach Brownies

Category : Appetizer, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:
"They look like brownies, but they're not sweet! Here's a wonderful way to have spinach. This is a treat my girls love to eat. Two 10 ounce packages frozen spinach, cooked, may be substituted for fresh." - Author

Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 35 Min
Servings: 24

Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) package spinach, washed in bag, rinsed and chopped
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 eggs, large
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, unsalted, melted
1 onion, chopped
1 (8 ounce) package mixed italian cheese, shredded
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
2. Lightly cook onions and garlic. Place spinach in saucepan, lower heat to simmer and cook until spinach is limp, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
3. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt and baking powder. Stir in eggs, milk and butter. Mix in spinach mixture and cheese.
4. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool before serving.
