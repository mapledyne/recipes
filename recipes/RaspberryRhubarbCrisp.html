Raspberry Rhubarb Crisp

Category : Dessert, Pie etc.

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
3 cups rhubarb, chopped into 1/2" pieces
2 cups raspberries
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract pure
salt
3 tbsp cornstarch
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
1 cup flour, all purpose
1/2 tsp cinnamon
salt
1 1/2 cup sugar, brown, packed

Instructions:
For the crisp:
•Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
•Spray 9x13" dish with cooking spray and set aside.
Make fruit filling:
•In a large bowl combine rhubarb, raspberries, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, salt, and cornstarch and toss to combine evenly.
•Pour into prepared baking pan.
Make the crisp:
•Combine butter with oats, flour, sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
•Stir well with spatula or even your fingers to make sure butter is well combined with dry ingredients.
•Sprinkle evenly over fruit filling.
•Bake in preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, until top is golden brown and fruit filling is bubbling.
•Let cool slightly for ~30 minutes.
•Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream. Enjoy!
