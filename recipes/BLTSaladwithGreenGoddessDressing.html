BLT Salad with Green Goddess Dressing

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 head of romaine, chopped
4 tomatoes, small ripe, chopped
a handful of fresh basil leaves, julienned
2-3 cups croutons, â€“ preferably homemade
3-4 slices bacon, cooked, sliced and precooked
chicken, grilled, (optional)
1 avocado, peeled and pitted
1 cup plain yogurt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 minced clove of garlic
1/2 cup cilantro and mint mixed, chopped

Instructions:
Salad: Mix everything together in a large salad bowl and enjoy.
Dressing (beginning with avocado): Crush garlic and mix it with salt. Let sit for 5 minutes. Blend salted garlic and remaining ingredients in a blender or food processor. Add pepper to taste, tabasco is not bad either, if it suits you.
