Butter Icing - Frosting

Category : Cake, Sauce

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, soft
3 cups sugar, confectioners', sifted
3 tablespoons cream, heavy
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla

Instructions:
Blend butter and sugar together. Stir in cream and vanilla until smooth. Makes frosting for two 8" or 9" layers or a 13" x 9" oblong.
