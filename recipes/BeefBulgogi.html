Beef, Bulgogi

Category : Grilled, Meat, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 5 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 lb steak, rib eye, sirloin or tenderloin, high quality
1/2 pear, asian or bosc
6 garlic cloves
1 small onion, yellow
2 teaspoons ginger, ground
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup mirin
1/4 teaspoons pepper black, freshly ground
2 tablespoons sesame oil, roasted
2 onions, green, finely sliced

Instructions:
Place the steak in the freezer for at least 30 minutes to make it easier to slice thinly. Remove the beef and cut into 1/4-inch slices at a diagonal to cut through the grain.
While the meat is chilling, place all the remaining ingredients except the green onions in a blender, and run on high until pureed. Pour the mixture into a Ziploc bag and add the meat slices and thinly sliced green onions, massaging the marinade into the beef. Let sit in the refrigerator for at least one hour.
Heat a grill, grill pan or griddle to high heat. Remove the beef from the marinade and lightly shake off any excess liquid before arranging on the cooktop. Grill each side for 1 to 2 minutes, until the meat has browned.
Serve immediately with rice.
