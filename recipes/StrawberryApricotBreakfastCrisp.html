Strawberry Apricot Breakfast Crisp

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour, hazelnut
1/2 cup rolled oats
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, salted, cold cut into chunks
1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
6 apricots, pitted and quartered
8 ounces strawberries, hulled and quartered
yogurt of your choice, for serving
(optional) lemon zest, finely grated, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat your oven to 400F. .
2. Add the hazelnut flour, oats, salt, cold butter, and honey and pulse in food processor just until a chunky mixture forms.
3. Combine the quartered apricots and strawberries in a pie plate. Drop spoonfuls of the topping evenly over the top of the fruit.
4. Bake in the oven until the fruit is bubbling and the topping has browned, about 30 minutes. Allow to cool, then store in the refrigerator and dole out for breakfasts over the following days.
5. Serve a scoop of crisp (you can warm it up a bit first, or eat it cold) with a couple scoops of yogurt and top with a little sprinkle of lemon zest.
