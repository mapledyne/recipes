Toast, Thick Crisp

Category : Appetizer, Kid Friendly, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time: 3 Min
Cook time: 5 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
bread, fresh or day old, sliced 3/4 inch thick
toppings (see note)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Slice bread 3/4 inch thick.
2. Place it on baking sheet or right on the oven rack and toast it for 5 minutes (toast should be nice and brown but still soft inside).
3. Eat right away with one of the suggested toppings.
Note of Topping Ideas:
1. Rub the top with a raw garlic clove (the rough surface of the toast will grate the garlic in), be generous with your best olive oil, and sprinkle with salt. Optional when they are in season - rub half a tomato onto the toast after the garlic, crushing some of the flesh and juice into the bread before pouring on the oil.
