Breadcrumbs, Oily

Category : Sauce

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
bread, stale (that still has some give)
olive oil
salt

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Carve the crust off a rustic loaf with a serrated knife and tear into 1.5 inch pieces. Grind coarsely in a food processor or blender, then toss in a bowl with plenty of olive oil and a sprinkle of salt.
The crumbs should be tasty and a bit oily but not soaked.
Spread on a baking sheet and bake for 7 minutes. Use a spatula to scoop the crumbs into a pile, stir them around a bit, then spread them back out. Put them back in the oven for 5 minutes and repeat with the spatula. Keep baking and stirring, until the crumbs are crisp and golden.
Use mainly for sprinkling over pasta, but also to sprinkle on chickpeas or other beans, or vegetable gratin, or shepherd's pie.
