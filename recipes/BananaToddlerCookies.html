Banana Toddler Cookies

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert, Kid Friendly, Snacks

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
3 bananas, very ripe, mashed
1/2 cup applesauce, Gravenstein (exact)
2 cups rolled oats, organic preferred
1/4 cup milk, almond
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon (#5530)
1/2 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions:
Combine all ingredients.
Form into cookies.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 - 20 minutes.
