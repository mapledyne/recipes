Harissa crab cakes

Category : Meat

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 scallions (green onions), finely choped
2 tablespoons oil, grapeseed
1 egg white
1 tablespoon sauce, harissa
8 oz crab meat
1 limes
pepper, freshly ground

Instructions:
Place the egg white, Harissa, cut scallions, and 10 turns of pepper in a large bowl.
Squeeze the crab meat over the sink to remove excess water, then add to the bowl. Mix everything together.
Sculpt the crab cakes: form the mixture into 4 tightly packed patties about 2 1/2" in diameter and 1/2" thick.
Pour 8" diameter circle (about 2 tablespoons) oil into a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. As soon as it starts to shimmer, move to the next step.
Add the crab cakes and keep track of the order added to the skillet - start at 12 o'clock and work clockwise.
Don't fuss with them. Let them cook in peace for 3 minutes, until nicely browned of the bottom, which will hold them together. Carefully turn them over in the order you put them down. Smooth them down to approximately equal thickness, then cook for another 3 minutes.
Remove to individual serving plates and squeeze lime juice on top. Don't hold back on the lime.
