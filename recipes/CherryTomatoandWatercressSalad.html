Cherry Tomato and Watercress Salad

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
12 tomatoes, cherry or grape, cut in half
2 handfuls watercress, washed and tough stems removed
4 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
salt
pepper, freshly ground
(optional) bread, white crusty (like ciabatta)

Instructions:
Place tomatoes in a bowl. Strew the watercress on top, and drizzle over the olive oil.
Add salt and pepper, toss gently, and eat with white crusty bread.
