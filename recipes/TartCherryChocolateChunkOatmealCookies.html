Tart Cherry Chocolate Chunk Oatmeal Cookies

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) butter, unsalted, room temperature
3/4 cup sugar, light brown, packed
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs, large
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup flour, all-purpose
1/2 cup flour, white whole wheat
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt, kosher, Diamond Crystal brand (exact)
2 cups oats, old-fashioned
1 1/2 cups chocolate, dark chunks
1 cup cherries, tart, dried

Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 350. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or silicon baking mats and set aside.
In the work bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment (or using a hand mixer), cream the butter and sugars together on medium speed for about 3 minutes, until light. With the mixer on low, add the eggs one at a time, blending until incorporated between additions and scraping the side of the bowl if necessary. Stir in the vanilla.
Meanwhile, whisk the all-purpose and whole wheat flours, baking soda, and salt together in a medium bowl. Add this to the work bowl and mix on low speed until the flour is just incorporated. Add the oats, chocolate, and cherries, and mix well.
Use an ice scream scoop to portion walnut-size knobs of cookie dough onto the baking sheets, about 12 per sheet, and bake until golden brown at the edges and set in the center, 12-15 minute. Cool 10 minutes on the baking sheets, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely. Repeat with the remaining dough.
