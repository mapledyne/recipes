Pasta with Cream Sauce Recipe

Category : Pasta, Side Dish

Description:
This beautiful pasta is well coated with a delicious garlic-cream sauce. It's a versatile dish that can be served as a side dish or a main dish. - Amy Sauser, Omaha, Nebraska

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 package, (16 ounces) bow tie pasta
1 onion, red , small, chopped
3 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
4 large garlic cloves, minced
3/4 cup broth, chicken
1 1/2 teaspoons basil, fresh, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons fresh oregano, minced
salt
pepper
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute onion in oil until tender. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer. Stir in the broth, basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil; cook for 8 minutes or until reduced by about half. Stir in cream.
2. Cook, uncovered, 8-10 minutes longer or until sauce is reduced to 1-1/4 cups. Drain pasta; toss with sauce.
