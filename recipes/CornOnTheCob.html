Corn On The Cob

Category : Side Dish, Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 3 Min
Cook time: 7 Min
Servings: 3

Ingredients:
3 ears corn on the cob, shucked
butter, unsalted, for garnish

Instructions:
Steam in boiling water for 6 - 8 minutes.
Alternately, grill over medium-high heat, rotating intermittently to cook all four sides, for 20 - 30 minutes total.
