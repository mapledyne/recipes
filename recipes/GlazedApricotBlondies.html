Glazed Apricot Blondies

Category : Dessert

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 50 Min
Servings: 20

Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, salted, softened
1 1/4 cup sugar, brown, packed
2 egg
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 1/4 cup flour, all-purpose
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup apricots, dried, chopped
optional 1/2 cup Pecans, chopped
1/2 cup sugar, powdered
2 tablespoons butter, salted, softened
2 teaspoons lemon juice

Instructions:
Line 8–inch square metal cake pan with parchment paper, leaving 1 inch extending over edges for handles. Set aside.
In large bowl, beat butter with sugar until fluffy; beat in eggs, one at a time. Beat in vanilla. In separate bowl, combine flour and salt; stir in apricots and pecans.
Baking center of 325° oven for 40 to 50 minutes or until cake tester entered in center comes out clean. Let cool in pan on rack just until slightly warm.
Glaze: in small bowl, stir together icing sugar, butter and lemon juice; spread over top. Let cool. Using parchment paper handles, lift out of pan. Peel off paper. Cut into squares.
Make ahead: cover and store at room temperature for up to two days or over wrap with heavy-duty foil and freeze for up to two weeks.
