Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole

Category : Breakfast

Description:
"I mostly use this as a breakfast casserole, but it's great anytime. May be served with or without diced ham. Quick and easy to make, not to mention delicious!" - Author

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook time: 1 H
Servings: 12

Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package potatoes, hash brown, frozen
8 ounces ham, cooked, diced
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of potato soup
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream, full fat
2 cups Cheddar cheese, sharp, shredded
1 1/2 cups Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
2. In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, cream of potato soup, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese. Spread evenly into prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
3. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly brown. Serve immediately.
