Corn, Fresh with Scallions, Jalepeno and Lime

Category : Veggie Side

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 13 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
3 ears corn on the cob
2 scallions
1/2 jalape√±o pepper, seeds removed
2 tablespoons water
salt
1 tablespoon lime juice, fresh

Instructions:
1. Cut the corn off the cob. Prepare the scallions and jalepeno.
2. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high until bubbling. Add the corn, scallions and jalepeno. Cook until the corn begins to brown and stick to the bottom of the pan, about 10 minutes, lowering the heat if necessary.
3. Add water and scrub the bottom of the pan with a spatula to dislodge the extremely delicious brown bits. When the water has boiled off, salt to taste.
4. Take off the heat, add lime juice. Serve immediately.
