Ravioli Filling: Broccoli and Feta Cheese

Category : Pasta

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
pinch crushed red pepper flakes
6 oz broccoli, cooked and chopped
12 oz cheese, feta, crumbled
1 oz cheese, Parmesan
pinch nutmeg
salt
black pepper, ground

Instructions:
Saute garlic in olive oil with red pepper flakes for 2 minutes. Toss with broccoli, feta, parmesan, nutmeg, eggs, salt and pepper.
Use this as a filling for ravioli pasta, following other directions to make the pasta itself.
Boil ravioli in water for 3 minutes.
