Quick Caramel Graham Cracker Bars

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert

Description:
I loved these at Emily Routledge's lovely memorial service.

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 48

Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar, brown
24 graham crackers, broken into rectangles
3/4 cup oats, quick cooking
1/2 cup coconut flakes, unsweetened
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 cup chocolate chips, semisweet

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly spray a jelly roll pan (10 x 15) with Pam cooking spray. Line the bottom of the pan with whole graham crackers. (You will need to cut some of the graham crackers to cover the pan.)
Heat the butter and brown sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat until it caramelizes and is well blended and about two minutes. Pour this “caramel” sauce over the graham crackers.
Mix oatmeal, coconut and walnuts in a small bowl. Sprinkle over the caramel sauce. Then sprinkle the chocolate chips over the top.
Bake 10 minutes, until the “caramel” sauce bubbles over the oatmeal mixture.
Let crackers cool before breaking into pieces to serve.
