Steak, Grilled

Category : Grilled, Meat, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 steak, top sirloin or tri-tip, 1 inch thick
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons onion powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon pepper
2 tablespoons butter
2 cloves garlic

Instructions:
Mix together the garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Generously apply the mixture to each side of the steak. I know it seems like a lot, but trust me, you'll be amazed. Give it a light brush with canola oil to help with the char.
Smash the garlic cloves into a ramekin with the butter. Microwave for about 20 seconds so that it melts.
Heat your grill to the highest it will go and close the lid. When it's hot, put the steak on the grill, close the lid, and let it grill for about 2 minutes. To get those nice crisscrossed grill marks on there, turn the steak 90 degrees and grill for another couple minutes. Remove and put the steak aside for a minute while the grill comes back up to full temperature with the lid closed. Then repeat the process on the other side. This should give you a medium-cooked steak.
Once done to your liking, let it rest under LOOSE foil for about 10 minutes.
Warm up the garlic butter and brush it lightly over the steak.
