Southern Slow Cooker Choucroute

Category : Slow or Pressure Cooker

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage, green, thinly sliced
1 onion, chopped
2 apples, tart (such as Granny Smith), cored and chopped
1 teaspoon bacon grease
1 garlic clove, minced
1 bay leaf
4 juniper berries, lightly crushed
3 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon mustard, whole-grain
1 pinch caraway seeds
1 pound smoked sausage links, halved

Instructions:
Add all the ingredients to the slow cooker. Salt minimally. Mix well and nestle the sausage halves among the cabbage, so they are less likely to dry out.
Cover and cook on low for at least 6 hours and as much as 8, stirring occasionally.
Taste and then salt again. Serve.
