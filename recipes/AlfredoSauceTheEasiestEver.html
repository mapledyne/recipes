Alfredo Sauce, The Easiest Ever

Category : Pasta, Sauce

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, unsalted
1 cup cream, heavy
1 cup Parmagiano Reggiano cheese, freshly grated plus additional for the table
Kosher or sea salt to taste
to serve: 3/4 to 1 lb hot cooked pasta, plus a splash of the starchy water it was cooked in
ground pepper, fresh
lemon zest, finely grated, optional

Instructions:
Melt the butter in the cream in a wide, heavy-bottomed saucepan over low heat, stirring or whisking often. When the butter is completely melted, add the cheese a little at a time, whisking or stirring after each addition. Continue cooking until the sauce is reduced and thickened, about 20-25 minutes.
Taste and add salt if needed. Toss with hot cooked pasta, adding a bit of the starchy pasta water to help it all come together. Serve in warmed pasta bowls, adding your choice of garnish, if desired. Pass additional grated cheese at the table.
