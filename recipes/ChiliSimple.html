Chili, Simple

Category : Beans, Soup, Weeknight

Description:
"This is the easiest chili recipe, and it is delicious and sure to please everyone. Using home canned tomatoes gives the best flavor, but is not necessary. Enjoy." - Author

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 20 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef
1/2 pound ground turkey
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons dried oregano
28 oz can tomatoes, canned, diced, Muir Glen brand
1 (14.5 ounce) cans tomatoes, canned, diced with basil, Muir Glen brand
1 (15 ounce) can garbonzo beans
(15 ounce) can kidney beans
1 onion, chopped
1 bunch cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Combine ground beef, turkey, onion, and garlic in large stockpot. Cook and stir over medium heat until beef is brown. Drain.
2. Stir in chili powder, salt, oregano, tomatoes, and tomato sauce; break up tomatoes while stirring. Heat to boiling, reduce heat to simmer, and cover. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 hour.
3. Stir in beans. Simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes; stir occasionally.
