Berbere Spice Dish

Category : Breakfast, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 30 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1 half of a large tomato, chopped
1 tablespoons berbere spice
1 - 2 cans beans or lentils, rinsed
6 eggs, scramble and cook until egg is cooked.
bread, white, for serving

Instructions:
Add tomato and onion to a frying pan in a tablespoon or so of oil (about med or med-high heat)
Add 1-2 tablespoons of berbere and cook everything together for at least 10 min (can cook for a long time on lower heat - thats how they seem to do it but I think 10-15 min is enough)
Add 1-2 cans of rinsed beans or lentils (I haven't found the exact type of beans they have, but similar to lima beans, but I have tried many kinds, whatever you prefer) to mixture in frying pan. cook for 5-10min, squishing the beans and mixing everything together. The amount of beans added should be about equal to the amount of onion/tomato mix.
On medium heat, add 6 eggs, scramble and cook until egg is cooked. This is about the serving size for one person so you can scale up or down.
Serve with white crusty bread or buns, tear of bites and pick up egg/berbere mix with the bread.
