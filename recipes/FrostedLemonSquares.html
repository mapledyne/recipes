Frosted Lemon Squares

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert

Description:
She cuts the squares first and then frosts each one.

Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 40 Min
Servings: 36

Ingredients:
2 cup flour, all purpose
1 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar, powdered
4 large eggs, whole
2 lemons, Juice from
4 tablespoons flour, all purpose
2 cups sugar, granulated
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup butter
3 cups sugar, powdered
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
3 tablespoons whipping cream
4 ounces cream cheese

Instructions:
Crust: Cream together the flour, butter and powdered sugar (may be mixed in food processor). Press into ungreased 13" x 9" pan. Bake at 350Â° for 20 minutes until light golden color.
Filling: Beat the eggs. Add lemon juice (use real lemons for greater flavor), flour, sugar and salt. Mix together. Pour over baked crust. Bake for 25 minutes until golden brown. Let cool completely before icing.
Icing: Cream the butter and sugar together. Add whipping cream and vanilla. Add the cream cheese into this mixture. (May all be done in food processor.) Spread icing on cooled lemon layer, allowing icing to harden a bit. Cut into squares.
