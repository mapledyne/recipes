Peaches, Chilled with Wine

Category : Dessert, Fruit

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
4 peaches, ripe, medium, rinsed and patted dry
2 tablespoons sugar, or more to taste
2 cups wine, crisp dry white or rose

Instructions:
Slice the peaches very thin (I aim for 12 - 16 slices per peach). Combine the peaches and sugar in a large storage container and toss gently to mix. Add the wine and toss gently again. Taste and if you like more sweetness, add sugar, a tablespoon at a time.
When they are sweetened to your liking, cover and refrigerate for several hours or up to 1 day.
Serve peaches very cold, straight from the refrigerator, in short, wide glasses with a small ladleful of their liquid. Eat the peaches, then drink the liquid left in the glass.
