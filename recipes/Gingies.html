Gingies

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert, Kid Friendly

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
6 cups flour, all purpose
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 tablespoon ginger, ground
1 tablespoon cinnamon, ground
1 teaspoon cloves, ground
1 teaspoon allspice, ground
1 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar, dark brown, packed
1 1/2 cup molasses, dark
2/3 cup water

Instructions:
1. Mix shortening, brown sugar an molasses thoroughly. Stir in water.
2. Blend all dry ingredients. Stir in. Chill.
3. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Roll 1/2" thick. Cut into cookies. Place far apart on a lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake about 15 minutes or until no imprint remains when touched lightly. Frost when cooled.
Better after a day or two.
