Cod with Parsley Sauce

Category : Seafood, Side Dish

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings:

Ingredients:
a little butter, salted
4 (1 inch thick) cod sticks
1 cup stock, fish or vegetable
a small bunch parsley, fresh
1/2 cup cream, heavy
salt
black pepper, freshly ground
(optional) squeeze lemon juice

Instructions:
Rub the inside of a shallow baking dish with the butter. Put the cod steaks in the dish and pour over the stock. Bake for 8-10 minutes in a preheated 450Â°F oven. Chop the parsley. Pour the stock from the fish into a shallow pan and return the fish, covered by a plate, to the switched-off oven to keep warm.
Reduce the stock over high heat to about half of its original volume. Pour in the cream, tip in the parsley, and leave to bubble over a high heat till slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.
Taste the sauce, adding salt and pepper and a squeeze of lemon juice if you want. Pour the sauce over and around the fish, and serve.
