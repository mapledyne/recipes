Celeriac, Potato and Apple Soup

Category : Soup, Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 1 H 20 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil, extra-virgin
1 onion, medium, chopped
2 leeks, halved lengthwise, cleaned and sliced
to taste salt
2 celeriac, peeled and diced
1 (~3/4 lb) potato, russet, peeled and diced
2 apples, peeled, cored and diced
2 quarts stock
1 sprig thyme, fresh
1 sprig parsley, fresh
to taste pepper, black, freshly ground

Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a large, heavy soup pot over medium heat and add the onion, leeks and a pinch of salt. Cook, stirring, until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add the celeriac and a generous pinch of salt, cover partially and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring often, until the celeriac has begun to soften.
Add the potatoes, apples, water or stock, salt to taste, and the thyme and parsley. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer 1 hour, or until the vegetables are very tender.
Blend the soup in batches in a blender, food processor, or use an immersion blender in the pot. The soup should be very smooth. Strain if desired. Return to the pot. Stir and taste. Adjust salt, add freshly ground pepper, and heat through.
