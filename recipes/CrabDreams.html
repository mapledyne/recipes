Crab Dreams

Category : Appetizer, Kid Friendly, Seafood, Weeknight

Description:
A Millington & Ball family favourite.

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 5

Ingredients:
1 can crab meat, canned
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 package of 6 English muffins, white
garnish paprika

Instructions:
1. Drain crab. Mix it with cream cheese and butter. Spread it on the English muffins (split in half). Garnish with paprika.
2. Heat in a 350 degree oven 12 - 15 minutes or until bubbly and warm. Let cool 3 - 5 minutes then serve.
