Cookies, Sugar

Category : Cookie or Bar, Dessert

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 10

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar, confectioners'
1 cup butter
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour, all-purpose
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cream of tartar

Instructions:
Mix sugar and butter. Add egg, flavourings and then dry ingredients.
Chill 2 - 3 hours.
Roll out and sprinkle with sugar and cut out.
Bake 7 - 8 minutes at 375 degrees.
