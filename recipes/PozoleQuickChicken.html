Pozole, Quick Chicken

Category : Soup

Description:


Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook time: 40 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra-virgin
2 pounds chicken thighs, boneless skinless
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon chile powder
1 teaspoon Salt and pepper, each
1 onion, large, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 carrots, peeled and diced
2 ribs celery, diced
1 chipotle, in adobe, finely chopped and two tablespoons the adobo sauce reserved
32 oz can hominy
8 cups chicken stock
1 can tomatoes and green chilies, diced
A selection of shredded cabbage, cilantro, cotija cheese, lime juice, sour cream, avocado, corn chip, for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat the oil over medium-high heat in large pot. Season the chicken with the cumin, chile powder, and salt and pepper. Brown each side of seasoned chicken (about 4 to 5 minutes per side), then remove chicken and set aside on a plate. Don't clean the pan just yet.
2. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery to pan, and saut√© 10 minutes, until softened.
3. Add the chipotle and adobo sauce, hominy, chicken stock, and tomatoes and chilies, and bring to a boil. Shred the reserved chicken, and add it back into the pot. Reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Serve with an assortment of toppings. We used shredded cabbage, cilantro, cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime. You can use sour cream, avocado, corn chips, jalapenos, onion, or anything you like, really. Make it yours!
