Tea, Iced Orange Mint

Category : Drinks

Description:
Most famous for its prevalence at Ben and Nick's wedding picnic. Divine!

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 10 Min
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
3 cups water
5 - 6 sprigs mint, fresh, well rinsed
1/3 cup sugar, white
2 cups orange juice, fresh
1/2 cup lemon juice, fresh
ice and water
1 lemon or orange, thinly sliced (optional)

Instructions:
Bring the 3 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan. Add mint, cover, remove from heat and steep 15 minutes or longer. Remove mint.
Add the sugar and stir until dissolved. The resulting concentrate may be cooled and/or frozen for later use.
When preparing, combine orange and lemon juices in a 1/2 gallon/2L serving pitcher. Add mint concentrate, water and ice to fill the pitcher. Chill completely and serve garnished with mint sprigs and thin slices of oranges and lemons (optional).
