Slaw, Citrus and Brussel Sprout

Category : Salad

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
1 grapefruit, supremed
2 clementines or oranges, supremed
1/4 cup grapefruit juice
4 kumquats, sliced
1/2 pound brussel sprouts, shredded
1/3 cup olives, green, chopped
4 dates, thinly sliced
3 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin

Instructions:
In a bowl, toss grapefruit and clementines (or oranges) with the grapefruit juice. Add sliced kumquats, brussel sprouts, olives, dates and olive oil.
Season with salt and pepper and serve.
