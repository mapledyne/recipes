Pumpkin Oreo Cheesecake Bars

Category : Dessert

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings:

Ingredients:
20 Oreos
2 1/2 tablespoons butter, unsalted, melted
2 8-oz. packages cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 cup pumpkin puree, canned
3 eggs, large
1 teaspoon vanilla extract [#8144]
3 tablespoons flour, all-purpose
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
salt
1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:
Instructions
1.Preheat oven to 350ÂºF. Line an 8-inch-square pan with foil, and lightly spray with non-stick cooking spray.
2.For the crust: crush the 20 cookies in food processor until small crumbs. Mix in butter. Press evenly into pan. Bake until firm, 10 to 12 minutes. Allow to cool slightly.
3.Make filling: with an electric mixer on medium speed, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Mix in pumpkin, and then the eggs, one at a time. Mix in the vanilla, flour, spice and salt.
4.Pour mixture into pan. Crumble the 5 extra chocolate sandwich cookies over the top of the filling. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over. Swirl slightly with a knife.
5.Bake 40 to 45 minutes, until cheesecake is set around edges but jiggles slightly in center.
6.Cool completely and cover with plastic wrap. Chill until firm, at least 3 hours. Cut into squares to serve
