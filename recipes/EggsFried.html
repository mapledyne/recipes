Eggs, Fried

Category : Breakfast

Description:


Prep Time:
Cook time: 5 Min
Servings: 1

Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 tablespoon butter
salt and black pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet that won't stick, to low. Cast iron preferred. Not too hot. Far better to start too cold than too hot for a tender egg.
2. Add a pat of butter and let it melt, then foam slightly, and then add the egg.
3. When the white - all of it - is set, flip the egg (or keep it sunny side up and don't). If an egg sandwich is what you are after, break the yolk before flipping and cook until just set, and your chin will stay unyolked as you bite in.
4. Salt and pepper
