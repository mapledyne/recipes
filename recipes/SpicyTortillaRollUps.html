Spicy Tortilla Roll-Ups

Category : Appetizer

Description:
"These are delicious, easy, and can be made ahead of time. No leftovers, either! I always serve them on a platter with hot sauce for dipping. You may serve them with toothpicks in each spiral for easy eating!!" - Author

Prep Time: 1 H 10 Min
Cook time:
Servings: 60

Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (2 ounce) can olives, black, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can green chiles, diced
1 (4 ounce) jar pimento peppers, sliced, drained
2 onions, green, minced
3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
3 tablespoons cilantro, fresh, chopped
10 tortillas, flour, 10 inch

Instructions:
1. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, olives, chiles, pimentos, green onions, hot sauce and fresh cilantro. Spread the mixture onto tortillas. Roll the tortillas up and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
2. Slice the roll ups and serve.
