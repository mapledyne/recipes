Strawberry Pie, The Very Best

Category : Dessert, Pie etc.

Description:


Prep Time: 30 Min
Cook time: 15 Min
Servings: 8

Ingredients:
1 cup flour, all purpose
1 tablespoon sugar
6 tablespoons butter, room temperature
1 egg yolk, large
8 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup sour cream, full fat
1 - 1.5 quarts strawberries, ripe flavorful
1 cup sugar
3 tablespoons cornstarch

Instructions:
Pie Shell:
Combine flour and sugar. Work butter with fingers. Add egg yolk and 1 tablespoon of ice water. Work until dough sticks together - don't overwork.
Pat into a flat round, wrap, and chill about half an hour, until firm. Roll between sheets of plastic wrap to fit a 9-inch tart pan. Press shell into pan. Chill.
Bake at 375 degrees for about 15 minutes, pricking with a fork to prevent bubbles. Let cool.
Filling:
Beat cream cheese till fluffy. Add sour cream and beat until smooth. Spread on bottom of pastry shell and refrigerate.
Wash and hull the berries. Mash enough uneven ones to make a cup. Force through a sieve and add water to make a cup.
Mix sugar and corn starch. Add a half-cup of water and sieved berries. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until mixture is clear and thickened. Boil for one minute. Remove from heat.
Fill shell with remaining berries, tips up. Pour mixture over top. Chill before serving.
