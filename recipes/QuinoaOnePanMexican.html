Quinoa, One Pan Mexican

Category : Christine Friendly, Grain or Rice Dishes, Vegetarian Main Dish, Weeknight

Description:


Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook time: 25 Min
Servings: 4

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil, extra virgin
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 jalapenos, minced
1 cup quinoa
1 cup vegetable broth
1 (15-ounce) can beans, black, drained and rinsed
1 (14.5 oz) can tomatoes, fire-roasted, diced
1 cup corn, frozen
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
salt, to taste
1 avocado, halved, seeded, peeled and diced
lime
2 tablespoons cilantro leaves, fresh, chopped
pepper, black, freshly ground

Instructions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add garlic and jalapeno, and cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Stir in quinoa, vegetable broth, beans, tomatoes, corn, chili powder and cumin; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer until quinoa is cooked through, about 20 minutes. Stir in avocado, lime juice and cilantro.
Serve immediately.
