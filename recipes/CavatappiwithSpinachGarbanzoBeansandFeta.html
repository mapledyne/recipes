Cavatappi with Spinach, Garbanzo Beans, and Feta

Category : Vegetarian Main Dish

Description:
"This is a very simple recipe, yet the flavors are deep and complex. You can substitute penne or any other short pasta for the cavatappi." - Author

Prep Time:
Cook time:
Servings: 6

Ingredients:
4 cups spinach, coarsely chopped
4 cups cavatappi pasta (or similar)
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/8 cup olive oil, extra virgin
1 tablespoon lemon juice, fresh
salt
black pepper
1 can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
2 cloves garlic, crushed
freshly ground black pepper
lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions:
Cook pasta to al dente.
Combine first 10 ingredients in a large bowl; toss well. Garnish with lemon wedges, if desired.
